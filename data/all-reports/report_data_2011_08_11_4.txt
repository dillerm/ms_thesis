H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
 In the Demnhoor district of Egypt, a 6-year-old girl developed symptoms of bird flu and was hospitalized on July 12.  She was diagnosed with H5N1 highly pathogenic influenza, treated with oseltamivir, an antiviral medication, recovered and was discharged from the hospital on July 30.  Investigations indicate that the 6-year-old had exposure to poultry suspected to be infected with bird flu.  Out of 151 cases of human infection of bird flu in Egypt, 52 have been fatal.
