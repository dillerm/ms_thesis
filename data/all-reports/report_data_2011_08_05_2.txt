H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
 In the Nghe An province of Vietnam, 5 villages have experienced outbreaks of H5N1 highly pathogenic avian influenza between July 17 and July 27.  Out of 9 deaths and 1,494 cases of bird flu, 2,517 birds have been culled to prevent the spread of disease.  The source of the outbreaks remains unknown.  Control measures taken include quarantine, movement control, and disinfection.  Measures yet to be applied in response to the outbreaks include screening, zoning, and vaccination.
