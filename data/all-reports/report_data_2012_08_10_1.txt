H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
In the Ninh Binh province of Vietnam, two outbreaks of H5N1 highly pathogenic avian influenza that occur at the end of last month were reported this week. Out of 1,480 birds, 577 died from the disease, while an additional 903 birds were culled to prevent the spread of the bird flu virus. The source of these outbreaks remains unknown. Control measures applied include quarantine and movement control of poultry and disinfection of affected areas. Further control measures that will be applied are screening for bird flu, zoning, and vaccination in response to the outbreaks.
