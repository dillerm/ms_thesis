Taiwan (HPAI H5N2)
The Animal Health Inspection and Protection Office confirmed a case of highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus at a chicken farm in Tainan.  After suspicious deaths were reported, the farm was quarantined, and samples were taken for laboratory testing.  The remaining 5,847 birds were culled to prevent further spread of the disease.  This is the 15th farm in Taiwan to be infected with avian influenza this year, the cause of this outbreak was not reported.
