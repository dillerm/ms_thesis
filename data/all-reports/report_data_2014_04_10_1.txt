Avian Influenza in Poultry in Bangladesh (HPAI H5N1?).
Bangladesh Health officials confirmed on March 28 that 7,000 chickens were culled at a government poultry farm in the district of Jessore after several birds were found dead and test results confirmed avian influenza. To prevent the disease from spreading further, 1,882 eggs, 1,300 kg of feed and 19 kg of vitamins were also destroyed. This outbreak has not been reported to OIE.
