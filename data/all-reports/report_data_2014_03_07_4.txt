Avian Influenza in Poultry in South Korea (HPAI H5N8).
Multiple outbreaks of H5N8 have been confirmed throughout South Korea, including one at a government-run facility research center. In total, about 315,213 birds have been destroyed, including 6,000 chickens and ducks from the research center. The source of the outbreaks remains unknown and the country remains on high alert as they hope to prevent further outbreaks of the virus.
