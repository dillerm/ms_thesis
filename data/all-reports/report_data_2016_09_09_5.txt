Avian Influenza in Humans in China (H9N2)
New human cases of H9N2 avian influenza virus have emerged in Jiangxi province and Yunnan province.  The patient in Jiangxi, a 4-year-old girl, was diagnosed on August 19.  The patient in Yunnan, a 10-month-old boy, was diagnosed on August 25.  Both children have recovered, it is unknown how they contracted the disease.
