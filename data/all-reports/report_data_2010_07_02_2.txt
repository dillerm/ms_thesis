H5N1 in Wild birds in Russia
A list of species for the mortality event reported last week involving 367 birds found dead on a lake in Ubsu Nur, in the Republic of Tuva has been released. The affected species included great crested grebe (Podiceps cristatus), goosander (Mergus merganser), grey heron (Ardea cinerea), gadwall (Anas strepera), Eurasian spoonbill (Platalea leucorodia).
