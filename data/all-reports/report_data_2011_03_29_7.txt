H5N1 infection in humans in Indonesia
  It was recently reported that a 26-year-old female resident of Desa Pacarejo, Semanu, Gunungkidul, died on March 23 after being treated for 3 days in Sardjito hospital, Yogyakarta, as a suspected bird flu patient. The Head of Health Service Kabupaten Gunungkidul, Dr. Sri Raharto, said the cause of death is still unknown. Currently, confirmation testing is being conducted by the Ministry of Health.
