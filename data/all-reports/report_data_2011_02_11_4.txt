H5N1 in Poultry in Cambodia
An outbreak of H5N1 bird flu in Kandal province was confirmed on January 29 after 48 birds died in a village. An investigation team from the National Veterinary Research Institute of the Department of Animal Health and Production conducted an investigation in the Prek Poung village and carried out surveillance in neighboring villages from January 31 to February 4. Starting on January 30 they culled 174 chickens in the area to prevent the disease from spreading. Other control measures such as movement control within the country and disinfection of the area have been enacted. The origin of the outbreak is unknown.
