H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
Thirty-five chickens and 150 ducks from a household 80 km north of Hanoi died; the birds tested positive for H5N1. Around 700 other ducks and chickens have been culled to contain the outbreak. H5N1 has been detected in a flock of young ducks in the Mekong delta province of Tra Vinh. The animal health department said that about 150 ducks are affected and the entire flock of 450 will be destroyed. A total of four provinces are currently affected by bird flu.
