Korea (HPAI H5N8)
According to a World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) report, 110 additional cases of highly pathogenic H5N8 avian influenza virus have been reported in the provinces of Chungcheongbuk-do, Chungcheongnam-do, Gyeonggi-do, Gyeongsangnam-do Jeollabuk-do, Jeollanam-do, and Ulsan Metropolitan City.  In total, 236 chickens and ducks died and the remaining 3,036,083 birds were culled to prevent the virus from spreading.  The source of the outbreak is unknown, and epidemiological investigations are still ongoing. Since the beginning of the currernt outbreak in 9/24/15, a total of 162 outbreaks have been detected. As the last detection (in South Jeolla) was in 6/10/15, the present situation is considered “stable” and the H5N8 outbreak over.
