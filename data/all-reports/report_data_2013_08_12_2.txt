India (H5N1)
Health Officials in India have issued an alert after two outbreaks of H5N1 were reported in the Indian state of Chhattisgarh.  Of the 8,442 birds, 2,856 died after contracting the virus.  Preventative measures being applied include stamping out, movement control, screening, and dipping and spraying.  Furthermore, areas near the poultry farms are being monitored and sanitized to prevent the disease from spreading further.
