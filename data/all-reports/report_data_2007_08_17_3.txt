H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
Bird flu has been detected in a farm in the northern province of Cao Bang. Eighty-nine chickens and ducks have died as of Aug 11 and samples sent to the Animal Health Department have been confirmed to be infected with H5N1. The farm is located about 70 km south of the border with the Chinese province of Guangxi. Cao Bang, which had remained free of bird flu this year, joins Dien Bien and Dong Thap as provinces under bird flu watch, down from a high of 18 provinces.
