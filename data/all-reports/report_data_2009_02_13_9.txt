H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
A 19-year-old man, from Jagapati in Badung, a village known for cock-fighting, has been placed under observation and is being treated for suspected bird flu. The man developed influenza-like symptoms on Feb. 9, a few days after bird flu was reported in the area. He has been placed on antiviral therapy and is said to be responding well. Samples have been sent for H5N1 testing. On Feb. 4, 15 birds suddenly died and the Badung Animal Husbandry and Fisheries Agency said that one out of every five birds in the village of Jagapati has tested positive for H5N1.
