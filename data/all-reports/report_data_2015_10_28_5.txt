Vietnam (HPAI H5N6) in WHO
The World Organisation for Animal Health Report (OIE) has reported two cases of highly pathogenic H5N6 avian influenza in backyard flocks in the Nam Dinh Province.  A total of 700 birds died and the remaining 3,059 were culled to prevent further spread of the virus.  In addition to culling, the practices of stamping out, quarantine, movement control, surveillance, and disinfection were carried out.  The source of the outbreaks is unknown.
