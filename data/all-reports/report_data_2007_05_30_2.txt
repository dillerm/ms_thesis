H5N1 in Poultry in China:
The Xinhua news agency said on May 19 that bird flu has  killed 11,172 poultry in Hunan province in central China.  The remaining  52,874 birds were destroyed, according to new reports. The date of the outbreak  is listed as May 12, and the birds were identified as ducklings in the official  report to the OIE.  The last previous outbreak in China occurred in March  in a poultry market in Lhasa, Tibet, where 680 chickens died.
