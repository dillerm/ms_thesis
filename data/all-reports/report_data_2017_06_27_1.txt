Finland (H5)
Highly pathogenic H5 avian influenza was detected in Sastamala, Western Finland Province (Lansi-Ja Sisa-Suomi) on June 2, 2017. The virus resulted in the culling of one whooper swan that was collected on May 26. The source of the outbreak is unknown and the event is considered resolved. Control measured applied include screening, vaccination prohibition, and no treatment of affected animals.
