H5N1 in poultry. in Israel
 More than 40,000 turkeys were scheduled to be slaughtered March 8 at Kibbutz Rosh Tsurim in Gush Etzion after the H5N1 bird flu was discovered in one of the the three sheds.  According to Veterinary Doctor Michelle Blaish, culling will not be carried out in other chicken sheds on the kibbutz because no abnormal activity was reported outside the one building.
