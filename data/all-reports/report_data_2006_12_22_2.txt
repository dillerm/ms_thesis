H5N1 in Domestic Animals
In Vietnam, 490 chickens and 2,033 ducks have died on December 6, 2006,   at the Khanh Hung Commune, in the province of Ca Mau located in the Mekong   delta. One day later, 3,550 ducks died in three villages in the Vinh Binh   Commune, in the adjacent province of Bac Lieu. H5N1 has been confirmed in birds   in both provinces. All of the deaths were in illegally hatched, unvaccinated   birds less than a month old.
