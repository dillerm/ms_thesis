H5N1 in Poultry in Netherlands (low path)
Low pathogenic H7 bird flu broke out on a farm in Noord-Brabant province that houses 28,000 chickens. The origin of the disease is unknown but all of the birds have been culled to prevent the spread of the disease. Other control measures such as screening and disinfection of the premises have been carried out. Tests were carried out by the Central Veterinary Institute.
