H5N1 in Poultry in China
.Earlier this week, the Chinese Ministry of Agriculture confirmed that the H5N1 avian flu virus was detected Zhangjiang, in the province of Guangdong.  Symptoms were first reported on September 11, and since then, 6,300 ducks have died and 14,050 have been infected.  To help reduce the risk of more outbreaks, all poultry in the contaminated area were culled and the area was disinfected.
