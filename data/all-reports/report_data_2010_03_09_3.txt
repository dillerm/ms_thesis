H5N1 in Poultry in Myanmar
An outbreak of bird flu began on February 19 in the Yangon region. A flock of 121 chickens and 5 ducks was affected. Two of the birds died and the rest were culled to prevent the spread of the disease. Samples confirm the presence of H5N1 bird flu. During surveillance, the Livestock Breeding and Veterinary Department received samples of dead chickens and information on unusual mortalities. A detailed investigation is underway.
