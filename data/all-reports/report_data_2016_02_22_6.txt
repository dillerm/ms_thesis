Vietnam (HPAI H5N6)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported an additional case of highly pathogenic H5N6 avian influenza virus in a backyard poultry flock in the province of Nghe An.  A total of 415 birds died, and the remaining 2,615 were culled to prevent further spread of the disease.  Other control measures include vaccination, disinfection, surveillance, and stamping out.  The source of this outbreak is unknown.
