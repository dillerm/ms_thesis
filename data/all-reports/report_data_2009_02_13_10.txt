H5N1 in Humans in Vietnam
A 23-year-old woman from Dam Ha District in Quang Ninh province is reported to be the second confirmed H5N1 human case this year. She developed symptoms on Jan. 28 and was admitted to hospital with severe respiratory symptoms on Feb. 5. Local health officials say that she had recent contacts with sick and dead chickens before developing symptoms. Of the 108 cases in Vietnam, 52 have been fatal. Three provinces in Mekong Delta, one central province of Nghe An, and the northern province of Quang Ninh are currently affected by bird flu.
