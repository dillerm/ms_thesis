Avian Influenza in Humans in Taiwan ex China (LPAI H7N9)
Taiwan's Centers for Disease Control (CDC) confirmed on April 22 the third imported case of H7N9 avian flu since last April.  The victim, a 44-year-old woman from China's Jiangsu province, arrived in Taiwan on April 17 as part of a tour group.  On April 18 she developed flu like symptoms and was hospitalized on April 20.
