H5N1 in Poultry in Ghana:
An outbreak of H5N1 in poultry was detected at the  Nungua Farm in the Tema Municipal District this week. Over  500 “birds kept under an intensive system of management” have died in  the outbreak and approximately 8,000 birds have been destroyed in an effort to  control its spread.  Ghana had previously  reported two other H5N1 outbreaks in 2007; the most recent, in Sunyani, two  weeks ago.
