H5N1 in Humans in Cambodia
 The Health Ministry of Cambodia and the World Health Organization reported on April 6 that an 11-year-old girl who died on March 31 tested positive for H5N1 bird flu virus. The child’s death brings the total number of Cambodian fatalities from the virus to four since the beginning of the year. “The girl is the 14th person in Cambodia to become infected with the H5N1 virus and the 12th to die from complications of the disease since 2003,” the statement said.
