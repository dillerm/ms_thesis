Nigeria (HPAI H5N1)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported 4 new cases of highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus at poultry farms in the states of Katsina, Plateau, and Zamfara.  A total of 3,795 birds died, and the remaining 32,289 were culled to prevent the disease spreading further.  Besides culling, the following control measures have been applied: movement control; disinfection; quarantine; surveillance; stamping out, and official destruction of animal products. Traceability will also be implemented. The source of these outbreaks is unknown, but poor farm biosecurity was noted.
