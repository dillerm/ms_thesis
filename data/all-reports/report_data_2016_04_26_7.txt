Taiwan (HPAI H5N2)
The World Orgainsation for Animal Health (OIE) reports 2 new cases of highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus on poultry farms in Chiayi County.  A total of 558 breeding geese died of the disease, confirmed by testing at the Animal Health Research Institute, and the remaining 2,997 were culled to prevent further spread of the disease.  Additional control measures include: movement control; screening, disinfection; quarantine; stamping out; and zoning.  The source of this outbreak is unknown.
