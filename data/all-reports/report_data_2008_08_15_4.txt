H5N1 in Humans in Vietnam
The Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development announced that bird flu has been detected in a farm in Binh Son district. Seventy ducks died and 150 became ill during the outbreak which occurred late last in July. H5N1 has been detected in some of the samples, the ministry said.Similarly, ducks from two farms in Ben Tre province have tested positive for H5N1 at the 6th Regional Animal Health Center, according to local reports.(more...)
