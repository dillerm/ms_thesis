H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
An 18-month-old boy is confirmed to be infected with H5N1, the Egyptian state news agency MENA said on Feb. 8. The boy, who lives in the upper Egpyt govenorate of Menia, developed symptoms on Feb. 6, but is currently in stable condition. According to reports, he was exposed to dead birds. This is the 55th human case in Egypt since 2006 and follows the announcement of an infection in another young boy in Suez last week.
