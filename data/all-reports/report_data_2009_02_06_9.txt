H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
A two-year-old baby has been hospitalized in Suez since Feb. 2 and was found to be infected with the bird flu virus, the Egyptian Health Ministry said. A spokesman for the ministry said the boy contracted the disease while playing with infected birds. The boy is said to be in a stable condition. This is the third human case in Egypt in 2009 and the 54th case in total.
