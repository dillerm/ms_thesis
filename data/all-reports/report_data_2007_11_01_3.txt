H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
Testing of 480 ducks and 80 chickens that died at 13 small farms in Cao Bang province have tested positive for H5N1. Bird flu is reported to have returned to the northern province of Nam Dinh, but it has yet to be officially confirmed by the Department of Agriculture. If confirmed, this is the third province (following Quang Tri and Cao Bang) to be currently affected.
