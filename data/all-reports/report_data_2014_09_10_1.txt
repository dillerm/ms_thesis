Avian Influenza in Poultry in Vietnam. (HPAI H5N6)
Two outbreaks of highly pathogenic avian flu of the H5N6 sub-type have been reported in central Vietnam in August 2014. The first was in Xuan My in Quang Tri province and began on 15 August and killed 350 birds of a flock of 1,550. The rest of the flock was destroyed. The second outbreak started on 23 August at Dong Nhon Nam in Quang Ngai province, where 200 birds died and the rest of the 1,110-bird flock has been destroyed.  Control measures include limiting movement of birds inside the country; plus screening, disinfection, and culling on infected premises/establishments.
