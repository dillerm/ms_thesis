H5N1 in Poultry in Switzerland
The Federal Veterinary Office says an expectation of low bird flu risk this winter is based on current risk and new scientific findings.  Switzerland says it expects the risk of bird flu in the country to be low this winter, and has not designated areas for heightened bio-security measures.  Switzerland has had positive cases of bird flu in the past, but the country is not prone to high levels of outbreaks and mortality events.
