H5N1 in Poultry in South Korea
 OIE recent reported 200 new cases of H5N1 bird flu in Osu, Yeongcheon-city, North Gyeongsang.  Since April 14, 130 chickens have died from the disease.  Infection was confirmed by PCR testing. The cause of the infection in the chickens is unknown, but researchers are screening other birds for the disease. In addition, they are applying other control measures, including culling, zoning, and limiting movement of animals within the country.
