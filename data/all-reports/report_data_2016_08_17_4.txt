Ghana (HPAI H5N1)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) reports cases of highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus in the province of Central and in the Greater Accra Region.  A total of 1,649 birds died of the disease, and the remaining 1,245 were culled to prevent further spread.  Additional control measures include movement control, disinfection and disinfestation, quarantine, and stamping out.  The source of these outbreaks is unknown.
