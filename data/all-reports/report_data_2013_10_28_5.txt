Cambodia (H5N1)
 The Ministry of Health of the Kingdom of Cambodia confirmed one new human case of H5N1 on October 22, bringing the total number of cases to 21 this year.  The most recent victim, an eight-year-old girl from the Baray district in Kampong Thom province, is currently in stable condition.  The girl had been in contact with dead poultry shortly before she become ill.  Due to the high number of avian flu cases this year, a nationwide public health education radio campaign launched at the start of October.  Additionally, public health education campaigns are being conducted in villages throughout the country reminding the public to wash their hands often, keep children away from poultry, and to stay away from sick or dead poultry.
