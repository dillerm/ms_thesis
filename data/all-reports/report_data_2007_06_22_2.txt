H5N1 in Wild Birds in Russia:
Genetic materials or antibodies to “bird flu” were  detected in 47 samples from wild birds collected in the Altai Territory and the  Ust-Ordyn Buryat Autonomous District, according to the Russian agriculture  watchdog group Rosselkhoznadzor. United Press International is reporting that  H5N1 was detected, but the virus subtype was not specified in the Russian TASS  news story and a second report from the Russian news source Regnum said only  that “the genetic material of virus A-H5 and the presence of antibodies in the  blood serum have been discovered.”
