H5N1 in Poultry in Iran
 In the Mazandaran Province of Iran, two outbreaks of highly pathogenic avian influenza were reported last month. Out of 2,682 birds, 345 died while an additional 2,337 were culled to prevent the spread of disease.  While the source of the outbreaks is currently under investigation, control measures taken include quarantine, movement control as well as control of wildlife reservoirs, screening, zoning, and disinfection.
