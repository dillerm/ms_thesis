Vietnam (HPAI H5N1, H5N6)
The Department of Animal Health declared an epidemic of the H5N1 avian flu in poultry in three communes of the Mekong delta provinces of Vinh Long and Tra Vinh and the H5N6 avian flu in flocks of quail in Tinh Ha Commune in the central province of Quang Ngai's Son Tinh District. The Health Ministry also proposed departments of agriculture and rural development to enhance supervision in a bid to early detect outbreaks of diseases and bird flu to stop spreading.
