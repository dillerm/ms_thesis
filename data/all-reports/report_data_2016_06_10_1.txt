Avian Influenza in Poultry in Egypt  (suspected H5N8)
On June 8, 2016, 500 samples from three Egyptian governorates: Beni Suef, Minya and Eastern, were sent to the laboratories of Research Institute of Animal Health for testing. Some local media had said that the H5N8 strain of avian influenza, which devastated the United States poultry industry in 2015, has been found for the first time in Egypt.The General Union of Poultry Producers asked the Minister of Agriculture Dr. Essam Fayed, to approve the disbursement of more than 5.1 million pounds for funding to protect the poultry industry in anticipation of a positive result.  High mortality rated in poultry farms has doubled in farms throughout the country.
