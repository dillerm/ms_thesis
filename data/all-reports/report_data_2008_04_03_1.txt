H5N1 in Wild birds in Switzerland
Additional information about the the H5N1 case reported last week on Lake Sempach: The duck that tested positive and showed no signs of illness was a common pochard and was one of 200 wild birds that have been tested since October 2007 as part of a routine surveillance program. The rest of the birds tested have been negative. This is the first H5N1 case in Switzerland since 2006. More details about this case can be found in the OIE Report.
