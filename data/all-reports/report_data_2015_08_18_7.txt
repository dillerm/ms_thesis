Vietnam (HPAI H5N1)
According to a World Organisation for Animal Health Report (OIE), an additional case of the highly pathogenic avian influenza HN1 is responsible for killing 852 birds at a backyard flock in Càng Long District of Trà Vinh Province. To prevent further spread of the virus the remaining 184 birds were culled, the location was disinfected and surveillance measures were enacted.  The source of the outbreak is unknown.
