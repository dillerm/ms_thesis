H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
The Department of Animal Health confirmed a bird flu outbreak in Nam Dinh province. The outbreak was discovered on a farm in Y Yen district on November 22. More than 2,000 ducks had died on another farm in the same district from November 16 to 18. Local authorities have culled nearly 1,600 birds so far.According to a November 27th report, in Nghe An province, Dien Chau district, nearly 600 birds infected with H5N1 bird flu were culled. Health officials sterilized the breeding facilities and isolated epidemic areas to prevent the spread of the disease. An outbreak of bird flu also occurred in Ha Tinh province, Cam Xuyen district.
