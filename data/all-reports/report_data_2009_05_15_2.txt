H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
The Department of Animal Health reported a bird flu outbreak on a local farm in the province of Dong Thap. The outbreak began on May 10 and killed 49 of 142 ducks on the farm. Local authorities have been working to curb the spread of the disease in the area.  H5N1 has also been detected in the Thanh Loi commune in the delta province of Vinh Long.  A farmer has reported that beginning on May 5, 250 ducks have died in his flock of 1,200. H5n1 has been confirmed from samples taken from the dead birds. Currently four provinces Thanh Hoa, Quang Ngai, Dong Thap, and Vinh Long are affected by bird flu.
