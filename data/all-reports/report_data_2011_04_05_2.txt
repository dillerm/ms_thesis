H5N1 in Poultry in South Korea
Recently, OIE reported 520 new cases of H5N1 bird flu, all from a poultry farm in Seosan, Yeoncheon city, North Gyeongsang.  All 520 birds died from the infection beginning on Mar 22.  The source of the outbreak is currently unknown.  Poultry in the affected farm and within a 500 m radius have since been culled and buried. In addition, disinfection and clinical surveillance within a 10 km radius of the affected farm have been intensified.
