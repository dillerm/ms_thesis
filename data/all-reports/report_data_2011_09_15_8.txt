H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
On August 30 national authorities observed an outbreak of H5N1 highly pathogenic avian influenza in the Quang Ngai Province of Vietnam. Both chicken and ducks died, totaling 690, while an additional 2,800 chicken and ducks were destroyed to prohibit the spread of the virus. Earlier in August, two outbreaks of H5N1 bird flu were observed in the Quang Tri Province of Vietnam. Statistics on birds affected and control measures taken have not been reported at this time.
