H5N1 in Poultry in Romania
H5N1 has been detected at a small farm at Murighiol, located in the Danube Delta approximately 250 km from the capital. A total of 31 birds died from a household flock of 80. H5N1 has been confirmed in some of the chickens that died, according to Radu Roatis, the head of the National Sanitary Veterinary Agency. Romania had 55 outbreaks of bird flu in 2005 and 150 in 2006. Romainia is the seventh country in the Europe affected this year, along with the Czech Republic, France, Germany, Hungary, Russia and the U.K.
