H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
The Ministry of Health and Population of Egypt reported that a 45-year-old woman from Menofia governorate is recovering from a case of H5N1 bird flu. The patient developed symptoms on February 10 and received oseltamivir treatment on February 17. The Central Public Health Laboratories confirmed the presence of the virus through laboratory testing on February 18. Epidemiological investigation into the source of infection indicate that the woman had exposure to backyard poultry. Of the 160 cases confirmed to date in Egypt, 55 have been fatal.
