H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh.
H5N1 has been reported in six farms in Jessore District, Barisal Division. Jessore is located 300 km to the west of the capital Dhaka, where H5N1 was detected in poultry earlier this year. Over 9000 chickens have been culled on a government-operated farm and on farms nearby. Bangladesh has culled 107,000 birds in nine districts since H5N1 was first detected in March this year.
