H5N1 in poultry. in Palestine
 2,000 new cases of H5N1 bird flu in Sealet al Harthieh, Jenin, West Bank, were reported to OIE this week.  All 2,000 birds, 65-day-old turkeys from a farm, died within 3 days with some respiratory signs.  The source of the outbreak is unknown. The turkey farm was the only one of its kind in the area; PCR testing showed that nearby farms were negative for the bird flu virus.
