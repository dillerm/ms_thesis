Taiwan (HPAI H5N2)
Two additional cases of the highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus have been reported at a duck farm in Yunlin County and a chicken farm in Pingtung County.  7,113 birds died and the remaining 15,522 were destroyed to prevent further spread of the disease.  Farms within a 3-km radius of the affected premises have been put under intensified surveillance.
