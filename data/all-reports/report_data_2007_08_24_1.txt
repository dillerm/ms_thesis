H5N1 in Wild birds in Egypt
Identification of an H7
				virus in wild birds causes concern among Egyptian officials. A migratory
				bird found in a pond in the delta govenorate of Shariqya has tested positive
				for the H7 avian influenza virus. Upon discovery, Hatem el-Gabali, the
				Egyptian Health Minister called Minister of Agriculture Amin Abbaza and
				suggested that the anti-bird flu committee should hold an emergency meeting
				to assess the situation. A sample of the virus will be sent abroad for
				further analysis.
