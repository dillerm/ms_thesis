USA (LPAI H7N8)
The Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) has confirmed 8 cases of low pathogenic H7N8 avian influenza virus in turkey flocks in Dubois, County, Indiana. After a case of highly pathogenic H7N8 avian influenza virus was detected in the area, 8 nearby flocks were confirmed, and one flock is suspected to have a related but low pathogenic strain of the virus.Â  Testing on the 9th flock is still ongoing. APHIS is monitoring the area closely, in order to identify any spread or mutation of the low pathogenic virus.
