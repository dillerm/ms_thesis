H5N1 in Humans in South Korea
Two additional cases of bird flu have been reported in Iksan in southwestern Korea; 2,500 chickens died at one farm and 450 at another, the Ministry for Food, Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries said. The farms are located 25 km from a location where H5N1 was confirmed last week. Also, a suspected case of bird flu has been reported in a duck farm in Nonsan in South Chungcheong province. This is the first time in the current outbreak where a central province in South Korea may be affected. The farm is located 213 km south of Seoul.
