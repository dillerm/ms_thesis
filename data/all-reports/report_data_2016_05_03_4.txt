China (H5N6)
The Hunan Province Disease Prevention and Control Center reports China's first pediatric case of H5N6 avian influenza virus.  The patient, an 11-year-old female from Zhuzhou City, first began displaying pneumonia-like symptoms on April 11, and on April 13 was admitted to the 13th Hunan Provincial Children's Hospital of Infectious Diseases.  Testing done on April 20 determined that avian influenza was the cause of the child's symptoms.  The child's condition has gradually improved, and she remains in isolation.
