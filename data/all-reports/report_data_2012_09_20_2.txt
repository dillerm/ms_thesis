H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
.Earlier this month, an outbreak of H5N1 occurred in the Quang Binh and Hoa Binh Provinces of Vietnam.  A total of 400 birds were destroyed to prevent the spread of the disease.  The source of these outbreaks remains unknown.  Control measures applied include quarantine, movement control, and disinfection, while control measures yet to be applied include screening and zoning to monitor any possible future outbreaks.
