H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh
Authorities in Bangladesh have culled about 10,500 chickens this year. Director of Bangladesh's Fisheries and Live Stock Department Muhammad Salehuddin Khan told Xinhua on Monday, "We have culled over 8,500 chickens in January after detecting avian influenza in some commercial farms and households across the country. This month we also culled around 2,000 birds including 1,200 on Feb. 9 after detecting the disease in two commercial firms at central Manikgonj and Narayangonj districts and one household in capital Dhaka."
