H5N1 in Poultry in Russia
At a farm in the Krasnodar Territories 410 birds have died and H5N1 has been identified as the cause, according to a spokesman for Rosselkoznadzor, Russia's animal and plant health watchdog agency. All 22,000 birds at the farm will be destroyed. Bird flu was previously detected in the same region in January. This is the third H5N1 outbreak in poultry in Russia this year.
