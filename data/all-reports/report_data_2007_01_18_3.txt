H5N1 in Humans
A 14-year old boy from Kalideres, near Tangerang in West Java, who tested positive for bird flu on January 7, 2007, has died.  He developed symptoms on the first day of the New Year.  He had possible contact with dead ducks, and had buried "by hand scores of his birds that had suddenly died."  A 37-year-old woman from Serpong town, on the outskirts of Tangerang, was hospitalized and diagnosed with H5N1 on January 8, 2007.  The possible source of her infection is under investigation.  She is known to have recently purchased a chicken and killed it at home. These two are Indonesia's 75th and 76th human cases.
