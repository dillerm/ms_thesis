China (LPAI H10N8)
Chinese health officials confirmed another human H10N8 death on February 13. The victim, a 75-year-old-man from Nanchang, the capital of Jiangxi, first developed flu-like symptoms on February 4 and died on February 8. Due to the extremely high number of deaths from avian influenza viruses already this year, health officials are urging the public to keep away from live poultry markets and wash hands regularly.
