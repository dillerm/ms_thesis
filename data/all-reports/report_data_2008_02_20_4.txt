H5N1 in Poultry in Laos
Bounkhouang Khamboundheuang, the head of the Department of Livestock and Fisheries, said that some 600 birds have died in the village of Nam Ma in Luang Namtha province and H5N1 has been detected from the samples. He said that poultry within 1 km of the village will be culled and movement of poultry and eggs from the village has been forbidden. Laos previously had H5N1 outbreaks in three provinces and in the capital of Vientiane, but have not had any new outbreaks since March of 2007.
