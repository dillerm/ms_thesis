Germany (LPAI)
Low pathogenic bird flu continues to spread in the state of North Rhine-Westphalia.  One farm with 7,640 birds reported 1 case on June 4 and two farms housing a total of 4,079 birds reported 7 cases on June 8. All 11,719 birds from the farms have been culled to prevent the spread of the disease. Other control measures applied include control of wildlife reservoirs, quarantine, movement control, screening, zoning, and disinfection of the premises. The source of the outbreak is unknown.
