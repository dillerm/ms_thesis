H5N1 in Wild birds in France.
Four ducks found dead on August 8 in Diane Capelle in the Moselle  region have tested positive for H5N1. Two “wild species” are reported in the  OIE report, but four "ducks" are reported in news sources. No  information on what species the birds are has been provided. Two swans found  dead at the end of  July in the same area have previously tested positive  for H5N1.
