H5N1 in Wild Birds in Pakistan:
Over 40 crows have been found dead in the city of Islamabad over the past week. Eight were tested and two have been found to be positive for H5N1.  According to one news report, Agriculture and Livestock Ministry spokesman Mohammed Afzal said that there is no evidence of any infection in poultry farms in the country; but during 2007, H5N1 was reported from chickens and peacocks in the capital?s twin city of Rawalpindi, Punjab, in Mansehra, Northwest Frontier and at a zoo in Islamabad.
