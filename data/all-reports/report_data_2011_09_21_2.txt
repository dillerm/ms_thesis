H5N1 in Poultry in Cambodia
 In the Banteay Meanchey Province of Cambodia, an outbreak of highly pathogenic avian influenza occurred on August 25. After 156 birds died, an additional 50 chickens and 30 ducks were culled to prohibit further circulation of the virus.  The National Veterinary Research Institute conducted an investigation of the infected areas from September 1 to September 12, but the source of the outbreak remains unknown. Control measures taken include disinfection and movement control.
