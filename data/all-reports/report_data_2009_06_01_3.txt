H5N1 in Human in Egypt
A four-year-old boy and a four-year-old girl from the Sharqeya governate in the Nile Delta were confirmed of contracting H5N1 bird flu on May 27. The girl fell ill on May 23 and the boy fell ill the next day both have been hospitalized and are in stable condition after being treated with Tamiflu. There have now been 76 human cases of bird flu in Egypt since the disease first appeared.
