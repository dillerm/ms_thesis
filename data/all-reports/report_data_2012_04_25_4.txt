H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
 The Ministry of Health and Population of Egypt notified the World Health Organization that a 36-year-old female from the Giza governorate was infected with H5N1 bird flu.  The woman developed symptoms after exposure to backyard poultry on April 1, was admitted to the hospital on April 7, and died the same day.  Out of 167 human cases of avian influenza in Egypt, 60 have proved fatal.
