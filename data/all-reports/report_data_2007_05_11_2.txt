H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh.
During the past week, H5N1 outbreaks have been reported in several regions in the country. Some 8500 chickens were culled in three farms in the Savar region; a farm in the Mirpur area in the capital city of Dhaka had tested positive, and a farm in the Magura district, 200 km to the southwest of the capital, was also infected. In all, Bangladesh has culled 132,000 chickens in 52 farms since the outbreaks began in March.
