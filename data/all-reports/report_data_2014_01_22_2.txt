Avian Influenza in Poultry in Vietnam (HPAI H5N1)
 The Vietnamese veterinary authorizes and the World Health Organization confirmed four outbreaks of H5N1 throughout Bac Ninh province on January 13.  Of the 9,787 susceptible birds, 2,600 died after becoming infected with the virus while the reaming 7,187 were destroyed to prevent the disease from spreading.  Various control measures have been applied including movement control, vaccination, and disinfection of contaminated areas.
