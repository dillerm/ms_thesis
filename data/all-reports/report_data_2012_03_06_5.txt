South Africa (H5N2)
Commercial farms that raise ostriches in Western Cape Province reported an outbreak of low pathogenic H5N2 on February 23. The outbreak began on January 13 and affected 662 of the 2,963 birds on the farm. The source of the outbreak is unknown, but there are plans to cull the remaining ostriches to prevent the disease from spreading. There are also plans to quarantine and screen birds in the area. The tests that confirmed the disease were conducted by Deltamune on February 21.
