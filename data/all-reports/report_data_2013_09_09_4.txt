Avian Influenza in Humans in Cambodia
 The World Health Organization confirmed on September 4th that a 15-month-old boy tested positive for H5N1, bringing the number of human cases to 18 so far this year.  The boy is currently being treated and is in stable condition.  Cambodian health officials are urging parents and guardians to keep a watchful eye on children as they “seem to be the most vulnerable” against this deadly virus.
