Taiwan (LPAI)
Low pathogenic bird flu was detected on a farm of 1,000 ducks in I-Lan province during active surveillance. The 20 infected ducks showed no clinical signs of illness. Laboratory tests at the Animal Health Research Institute were positive for H5N2 bird flu. An epidemiological investigation of surrounding farms was conducted. No other cases were found and the origin of the outbreak is unknown. Precautionary measures, such as movement control, quarantine, screening, zoning, and disinfection of the premises have been undertaken.
