H5N1 in wild birds in Japan
OIE recently reported 3 new cases of H5N1 bird flu in wild birds in Japan: 1 in Aomori and 2 in Shimane. These birds, 1 peregrine falcon (Falco peregrines), 1 pochard (Aythya ferina), and 1 tufted duck (Aythya fuligula), were all found dead between March 10 and March 17 and have tested positive for H5N1 bird flu. The source of the outbreak is unknown, but wild birds are being screened for the disease.
