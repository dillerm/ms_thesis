H5N1 in Poultry in Egypt
An outbreak of highly pathogenic avian influenza occurred in the Egyptian governorate of Beni Suef this month. Both chicken and ducks tested positive for H5N1 on August 14 at the National Laboratory for Veterinary Quality Control on Poultry Production. Out of 35 chicken and ducks, 33 died, and all 35 birds were destroyed to prevent the spread of disease.
