H5N1 in Poultry in Cambodia
 In the Battambang Province of Cambodia, an outbreak of highly pathogenic avian influenza was reported earlier this month on a farm.  Out of 5,206 birds, 1,050 died while an additional 4,156 chickens, ducks, geese and broilers were culled to prevent the spread of disease.  The source of the outbreak remains unknown.  Control measures applied include movement control, dipping, and spraying.
