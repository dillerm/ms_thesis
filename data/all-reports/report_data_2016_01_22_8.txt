Vietnam (HPAI H5N6)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported an additional case of highly pathogenic H5N6 avian influenza virus in a backyard flock in the Lang Son province. A total of 155 birds died, and the remaining 65 were culled to prevent further spread of the disease. Besides culling, the control measures include disinfection, surveillance, and stamping out. The source of this outbreak is unknown.
