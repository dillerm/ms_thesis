H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
The Department of Animal Health reported on June 28 that bird flu had broken out on a local farm in Thai Nguyen province. Local authorities have culled the entire flock of over 530 infected ducks, sterilized affected areas, and placed restraints on the movement of poultry and poultry products in order to prevent the spread of the disease. This was the first bird flu outbreak in the province since early this year.
