H5N1 in Poultry in Indonesia
The village of Sidomakmur in the sub-district of Way Panji in Lampung Province has been named a bird flu endemic area since 17 chickens and 4 swans died there suddenly.  Rapid tests by the Livestock Services confirmed bird flu.Fifty percent of the Indramayu district inWest Java province was named a bird flu endemic area. Within last two weeks there have been several bird flu cases. The virus is considered endemic in 18 out of the 31 subdistricts. According to Nanang Hidayat, the Head of Distankan Kabupaten Indramayu, the crucial steps to suppress loss are to enhance disinfection, vaccination and public socialization.
