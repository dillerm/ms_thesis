H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
A 4-year-old patient with suspected bird flu tested negative for the disease by rapid testing, but M. Djamil hospital is waiting for the results of a test at the Center of Health Research and Development laboratory in Jakarta to confirm that the disease is not present. The patient has improved and is breathing normally. The child has been a patient at the hospital in Padang since April 20 and developed a fever along with difficulty breathing after contact with a dead chicken. The chicken has tested positive for H5N1 according to some reports.
