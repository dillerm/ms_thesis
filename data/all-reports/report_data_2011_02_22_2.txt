H5N1 in Wild Birds in Japan
Five outbreaks of bird flu in wild birds Japan have recently been reported to OIE: two in Miyazaki, one in Hokkaido, one in Nagasaki, and one in Yamaguchi prefecture. A total of 6 birds were found dead including 3 mandarin ducks, 1 peregrine falcon, 1 whooper swan, and 1 tufted duck. The origin of the outbreak is unknown.
