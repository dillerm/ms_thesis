H5N1 in Poultry in Spain
Low pathogenic H5 bird flu broke out on a farm in Los Arcos, Navarra Province on June 12. None of the 3,625 ducks on the farm showed clinical signs, but the virus was detected by PCR. The N1 subtype was ruled out, but no NA subtype has been determined. The outbreak is ongoing, but the premises have been disinfected to prevent the spread of the disease.
