H5N1 in Humans in Vietnam
The 4th case of H5N1 bird flu infection in a human in Vietnam was reported on March 7 by Dr. Tran Ngoc Huu, President of the Pasteur Institute in Ho Chi Minh City (HCMC). Doctors from the General Hospital in Dan Lak province transferred a 32-year-old man to the Tropical Disease Hospital in HCMC for suspected bird flu. The Tropical Disease Hospital put the man on a respirator for severe pneumonia and the Pasteur Institute conducted tests that turned out to be positive for H5N1 bird flu. It is not known how the man contracted the disease.
