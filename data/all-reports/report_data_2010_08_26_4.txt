H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
On August 25, the Health Ministry announced a new case of H5N1 bird flu. The patient is a woman from Qalyubiya governorate. She contracted the virus through contact with infected poultry and is being treated for high fever and breathing difficulty at Cairo hospital. She is in critical condition even after treatment with Tamiflu. This is the 122nd confirmed human case of H5N1 in Egypt.
