H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
In Soc Trang province, an outbreak of H5N1 bird flu occurred in a flock of 630 ducks. Five hundred ducks died and the rest were culled to prevent the spread of the disease. Bird flu also recurred on a farm in Nam Dinh province. Provincial health officials killed all 270 ducks on the farm in order to contain the disease. Dozens of bird flu outbreaks have been detected in Ca Mau province since the beginning of the year and a number of measures have been put in place to stop the disease from spreading. More than 5,000 birds have been culled as a result of these outbreaks.
