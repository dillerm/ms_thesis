H5N1 in Poultry in Egypt
According to a report on December 7, field tests carried out by the Directorate have discovered 89 domestic poultry breeding locations that are positive for bird flu. Infected birds have been culled and nearby birds have been vaccinated to prevent the spread of the disease. Engineer Sami Amara, Governor of Menoufiya, urges more severe measures, such as preventing the circulation of live birds and emphasizing education of citizens.
