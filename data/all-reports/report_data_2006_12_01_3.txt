H5N1 in Domestic Animals
Egypt has detected a new outbreak of H5N1 in the city of Luxor. The Health Ministry said that the virus was detected from domestic birds at the city's Najaa al Abayda laboratory. According to Abdel Rahman Shahhin, the ministry's spokesman, the area has been quarantined and the birds will be culled. Egypt last reported an H5N1 case in October, when an Egyptian woman from the village of Samanoud in the Gharbiya governorate died of the virus.
