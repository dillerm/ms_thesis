Taiwan (HPAI H5N2)
Highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza was confirmed in Taipei, Taiwan on June 23, 2017. Six total outbreaks were confirmed and over 55,000 birds have been culled. The source of the outbreak is unknown and the event is considered ongoing. Control measures applied include movement control inside the country, screening, quarantine, stamping out, zoning, vaccination prohibition, and no treatment of affected animals. The animal inspection authorities have said they strengthened quarantine and sanitation measures on nearby farms to prevent further infections.
