H7N7 in Poultry in Germany
German officials confirmed an outbreak of low pathogenic avian influenza H7N7 on a farm in Emsland in Lower Saxony, Germany, earlier this week. Of the flock of 13,406, there were 400 birds that tested positive with the virus. All birds at the farm were destroyed to prevent the disease from spreading further. The source of the outbreak remains unknown and control measures being applied include stamping out, zoning, and disinfection of contaminated areas.
