Palestinian Territories (HPAI H5N1)
According to the Israeli Agriculture Ministry, an outbreak of highly pathogenic avian influenza H5N1 virus was discovered in the village of Katana near Jerusalem at a small backyard farm with egg-laying hens.  Facilities within a 10 km radius are under inspection, and heightened supervision at crossing points between the Palestinian Authority and Israel is underway.  Avian influenza was also been identified in January and February at a farm in Moshav Avial in Israel and nearby Palestinian Territories.
