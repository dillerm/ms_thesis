Taiwan (HPAI H5N2)
Three additional cases of the highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus have been reported at a poultry farm in Changhua County, a turkey farm in Yunlin County, and a goose farm in Kaohsiung.  A total of 5.527 birds died, and the remaining 20,113 were culled to prevent the virus from spreading.  In addition to culling, the affected premises have been quarantined and farms within a 3 km radius are under intensified surveillance.
