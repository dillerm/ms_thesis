H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
Bird flu was detected in Thai Nguyen province in late December, according to the Veterinary Department of the province. Over 100 ducks and chickens died at two farms in northern Vietnam, and 128 non-vaccinated Muscovy ducks died and 20 were destroyed in a nearby outbreak that began on December 25th, 2008. This was the second outbreak in the province in 2008.
