H5N1 in Poultry in Bhutan
 According to the National Veterinary Laboratory, a total of 13 outbreaks of H5N1 have been confirmed in four provinces throughout Bhutan:  Chhukha, Thimphu, Mongar, and Dagana. Out of 8,599 birds, 412 died while the remaining 6,623 were culled to prevent further spread of the virus.  The source of these outbreaks remains unknown.  Various control measures being applied include quarantine, movement control, screening, and disinfection of contaminated areas.
