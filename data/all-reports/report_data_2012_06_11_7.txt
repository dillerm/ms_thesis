H5N1 in Humans in Cambodia
On May 26 in the Kampong Speu province of Cambodia, a 10-year-old girl has died after contracting the H5H1 strain of avian influenza. According to Cambodian Health Officials, the girl contracted the virus on May 20 after coming into contact with infected chickens in her home village of Pring, Bosedth district. Prior to the diagnosis from the Cambodian Pasteur Institute, the girl exhibited symptoms of fever and shortness of breath. The girl was admitted to the Kantha Bopha Hospital in Phnom Penh on May 25. This case is the third avian influenza fatality in Cambodia this year.
