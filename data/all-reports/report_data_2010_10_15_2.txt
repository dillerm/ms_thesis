H5N1 in Poultry in Egypt
A total of 18 highly pathogenic bird flu outbreaks were reported in 7 governorates in September; 2 in Beni Suef, 6 in Fayoum, 1 in Giza, 6 in Menoufia, and 3 in Qualyoubiyah. Most of the outbreaks were in household poultry, but one in Qualyoubiyah was on a commercial farm where the poultry had been vaccinated.
