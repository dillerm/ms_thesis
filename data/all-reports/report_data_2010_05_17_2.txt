H5N1 in Poultry in Indonesia
Nano Sudiyo Wiradat, Head of Distanak Kabupaten Merangin reported that bird flu has been found in chickens in Jambi province for over a year. Recently the disease has also affected ducks.  According to Nano, “We collected serum from duck populations, and several samples showed positive avian influenza.” He pointed out that ducks might survive and spread the disease, while chickens usually die within a few hours of infection.
