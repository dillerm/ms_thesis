H5N1 in Poultry in China
The official news agency Xinhua reports that an unnamed official said that the unusually warm winter has brought more migratory birds to the area near Turpan and this increases the chance of a bird flu outbreak. An H5N1 outbreak in Turpan, in northwestern China, was announced last week. "The wild birds ought to have flown south, but stayed near the city because of the warm weather this winter," Xinhua quoted the official as saying. "This has added to infection risks and made the control of the disease more difficult." (more...)
