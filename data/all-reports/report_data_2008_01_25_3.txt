H5N1 in Poultry in Germany
German authorities reported that H5N1 has been confirmed at a backyard flock in Bensdorf, Brandenburg. The possible presence of H5N1 was initially reported on December 14 by the provincial laboraotories and has now been confirmed by the national lab. The remaining 28 birds in the private flock, which was kept for private consumption with no record of trading activity, has been destroyed. (more...)
