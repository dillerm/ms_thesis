Bangladesh (HPAI H5)
Highly pathogenic H5 avian influenza has been detected in Dhaka Bangladesh. The outbreak of the virus resulted in the death of 124 house crows; 42 additional crows were culled. The virus was first detected on January 14, 2017, with a previous outbreak occurring in February, 2016. The source of the outbreak is unknown but the outbreak has been declared resolved as of March 16, 2017.
