H5N1 in Domestic Animals in Thailand:
H5N1 has been confirmed in six household chickens in  the central province of Thailand.  The remaining 10 chickens and all  poultry within 200 m are to be culled.  This is the third H5N1 outbreak  this year in Thailand.  The other outbreaks this year have been in the  provinces of Nong Khai and in Phitsanulok.
