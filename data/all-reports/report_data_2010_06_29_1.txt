H5N1 in Wild Birds in Russia
A mortality event involving 367 wild birds found dead on a lake in Ubsu Nur, in the Republic of Tuva, was reported to the World Organization for Animal Health (OIE). The birds were found on Jun 5, and the All Russian Research Institute for Animal Health (FGI-ARRIAH) has detected H5N1 in the samples. The affected species included Great Crested Grebe ( Podiceps cristatus ), Goosander ( Mergus merganser ), Grey Heron ( Ardea cinerea ), Gadwall ( Anas strepera ), Eurasian Spoonbill ( Platalea leucorodi a ).
