Avian Influenza in Poultry in China (HPAI H5N1)
 The Chinese Ministry of Agriculture confirmed an outbreak of H5N1 at a farm in Hubei Province on January 14.  Of the 50,000 susceptible chickens, 3,200 died after contracting the disease while the remaining 46,800 were destroyed.  To prevent the disease from spreading, control measures being applied include movement control, screening and zoning, and sterilization of the infected area.
