H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
Two human cases of bird flu have been confirmed by the Ministry of Health. A 13-year-old boy from Alexandria Governorate began to show symptoms on September 13 and was hospitalized September 14. He was treated with oseltamivir and is in stable condition. A 14-month-old girl from Behira Governorate began to show symptoms on Semptember 23 and was admitted to Damanhur Fever Hospital. She was also treated with oseltamivir and is in stable condition. Both have had a history of exposure to dead or sick poultry.
