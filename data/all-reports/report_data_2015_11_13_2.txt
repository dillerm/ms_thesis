South Africa (LPAI H7)
The World Organisation for Animal Health Report (OIE) has reported a case of low pathogenic LPAI H7 avian influenza virus on an ostrich farm in the Eastern Cape Province, South Africa with 1,200 birds.  The neuraminidase subtype was not reported.  The birds were tested prior to slaughter.  The outbreak was stated to have begun on 8/25/15, and testing was completed on 9/17/15, Screening and quarantine measures were implemented to prevent further spread of the disease.
