South Africa (LPAI H5N2)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) reports 2 cases of low pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus on commercial ostrich farms in Western Cape Province from outbreaks that began in February and March.  A total of 847 out of ostriches have been infected, with no fatalities.  Applied measures to control the disease include movement control, screening, and quarantine.  The cause of this outbreak is unknown.
