H5N1 in Poultry in China
 An outbreak of H5N1 bird flu among several chicken farms in the northwestern Chinese region of Ningxia was recently reported.  Last Friday, 23,880 chickens showed symptoms of avian influenza; of those, 10,468 died later. Test results from the National Avian Influenza Reference Laboratory confirmed the birds were infected with highly pathogenic avian influenza, and an additional 84,532 chickens were slaughtered to prevent the spread of the virus. Other control measures taken include quarantine, movement control, zoning, and disinfection of affected areas.
