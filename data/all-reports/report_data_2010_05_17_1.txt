H5N1 in Wild birds in Mongolia
On May 3, an outbreak of H5N1 bird flu in 26 whooper swans and greylag geese began in Sukhbaatar province. All 26 birds died from the disease, but it is unknown how many other wild birds may have been exposed. The source of the outbreak is unknown, but control measures such as quarantine, screening, and zoning have been undertaken. Samples from the birds tested positive for bird flu at the State Central Veterinary Laboratory.
