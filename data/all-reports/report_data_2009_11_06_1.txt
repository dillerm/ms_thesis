H5N1 in Poultry in Russia
 The highly pathogenic avian influenza H5N1 virus has been detected in a pigeon that was found on a balcony in a block of apartments in Pushchino, near Moscow.  The bird was found on October 26 and the All-Russian Research Institute for Animal Health has confirmed that the virus is H5N1. Russia has not had an H5N1 outbreak since June when H5N1 was detected in a die-off of grebes in Ubsu Nur in Tuva.
