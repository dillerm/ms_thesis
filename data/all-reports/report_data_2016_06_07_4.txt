Indonesia (HPAI H5N1)
The Agriculture and Food Sovereignty office in East Jakarta reports that city administration officials in the Rambutan subdistrict of the Ciracas district, confirmed that two out of five birds have tested positive for bird flu. A total of 113 chickens had died and the owner reported the case to the subdistrict officer. Government workers disassembled the remaining chicken coops and destroyed the remaining chickens to prevent the possible spread of the virus. The cause of the outbreak is unknown.
