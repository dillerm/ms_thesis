Ghana (HPAI H5N1)
Two additional cases of the highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus have been reported in the Greater Accra region at a backyard farm in the Accra Metropolitan Assembly with 68 cockerels and at a commercial poultry farm in Dangbe East.  A total of 183 birds died, and the remaining 1,799 birds were culled to prevent the disease from spreading.  The affected premises have also been quarantined and movement control measures put in place.
