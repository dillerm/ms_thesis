Taiwan (LPAI H5N2)
According to a World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) report, three new outbreaks of the low pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus have been reported at poultry farms in Chiayi County, Hsinchu County, and Taoyuan County.  A total of 25,573 birds died and the remaining 115, 627 were culled to prevent the virus from spreading.  In addition to culling, the affected premises have been placed under movement restrictions and farms within a 3-km radius are under intensified surveillance.
