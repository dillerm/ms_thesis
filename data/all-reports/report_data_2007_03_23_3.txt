H5N1 in Wild Birds in Russia:
Specialists from the Federal Veterinarian and Phytosanitary Control Service (Rosselkhoznadzor) have begun shooting wild birds in the Kaliningrad region to stop bird flu, according to press secretary Yevgeny Safronov.  The region did not have any H5N1 cases in 2006 and initial analysis of blood samples from the shot birds was also negative. (more...)
