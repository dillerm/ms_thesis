H5N1 in Poultry in Egypt
There were a total of eight cases of H5N1 bird flu in five governorates during June: 1 in Dakahlia, 2 in Fayoum, 1 in Menoufia, 1 in Qalioubiya, and 3 in Sixth of October. Most of the outbreaks were in household poultry; one occurred on a commercial farm in Dakahlia governorate.
