South Africa (LPAI).
A total of 5 ostrich farms in the Western Cape Province were subjected to outbreaks of Low Pathogenic Avian Influenza between March and June of this year. Out of 3,366 cases of bird flu, only 8 birds died from the disease, while 3,508 birds from a total of 9,005 susceptible birds were culled to prevent the spread of the disease. Control measures included quarantine and screening. The source of the outbreak remains unknown.
