H5N1 in Humans in Cambodia
 The World Health Organization (WHO) confirmed three new human cases of H5N1 since the start of the year, two of them being fatal, in the southeastern Takeo province and in central Kampong Speu province of Cambodia.  The victims, a 15-year-old girl and a 35-year-old man, died after being hospitalized with bird flu.  An eight-month-old boy was also treated and survived.  Last year, Cambodia reported three cases of H5N1, all of them fatal.
