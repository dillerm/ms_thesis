H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh
Eleven new outbreaks of bird flu were reported in March and early April in the districts of Gaibandha, Chittagong, Rajshahi, Comilla, Patuakhali, Biral, Narayanganj, Rangpur, and Norsingdi. Eight of the outbreaks occurred at commercial poultry farms and three at backyard breeders.
