Vietnam (HPAI H5N6)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported two cases of highly pathogenic H5N6 avian influenza in Vietnam.  The first outbreak began on January 5 in the Quang Ngai province where 454 birds were found dead and 546 were destroyed.  The second outbreak occurred in the Kon Tum province on January 6.  Local authorities tested samples from 550 dead chickens and 440 dead geese and burned the carcasses of all infected birds.  Other control measures include stamping out, disinfection, and surveillance within and outside the containment zone.  The source of the outbreak is unknown.
