UK (LPAI H5N1)
According to the Scottish Government, low pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza was identified in chickens on a broiler-breeder farm in Fife, Scotland on January 11.  The infection was detected following tests on several dead birds on the premises.  On January 13, 400,000 birds were culled.  A one kilometer control zone has been placed around the farm restricting the movement of poultry, carcasses, eggs, used poultry litter and manure, and restrictions on bird gatherings.  The source of the outbreak is unknown.
