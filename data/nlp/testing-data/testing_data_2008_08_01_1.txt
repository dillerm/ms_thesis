H5N1 in Wild birds in South Korea
A domestic cat has tested positive for bird flu. The cat died last week near the Mangyeong River in Gimje, and the Ministry for Food, Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries has confirmed that the cat died from avian influenza H5N1. This case marks the first mammal to die from the disease in South Korea, and any plans for future culling of cats, or other animals, is unknown.
