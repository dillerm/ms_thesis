H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
Bird flu outbreaks are ongoing in Ca Mau, Dien Bien, Soc Trang, Nam Dinh, Tuyen Quang, Ha Giang and Khanh Hoa provinces. In Khanh Hoa, 6,770 birds have died in two districts. The Veterinary Department acting director, Hoang Van Nam, reported on March 2 that there was still a high risk of the disease spreading to other localities, especially in the Mekong Delta region. The outbreaks in Tuyen Quang and Ha Giang were reported by the Veterinary Department on March 3, while the disease was first confirmed in birds in Tuyen Quang province on February 26. As of March 1, it was confirmed that 100 chickens were infected; most died from the virus. Health care agencies and local authorities are working together to prevent the spread of the disease.
