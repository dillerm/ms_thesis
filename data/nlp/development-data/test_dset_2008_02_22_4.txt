H5N1 in Poultry in Thailand
Researchers analyzing the virus that caused outbreaks in Pichit and Nakorn Sawan have found that the new H5N1 virus shows mutations that indicate gradual change from viruses that caused earlier outbreaks, according to Sakchai Sriboonsue, the director general of the Livestock Department. He stressed that the mutations did not indicate that the virus has become more pathogenic to animals or people; however, the continued evolution of the H5N1 virus might indicate that the virus continues to circulate in the region.(more...)
