H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
An eight-year old boy has been infected with H5N1, the Egyptian Ministry of Health has announced. The boy, a resident of Fayum, had a high fever, respiratory difficulties and was initially treated at a local hospital before being transferred to a specialty hospital in Cairo on November 8th. Abdel Rahman Shaheen, a spokesman for the Health Ministry, said the boy was exposed to the virus by infected poultry.
