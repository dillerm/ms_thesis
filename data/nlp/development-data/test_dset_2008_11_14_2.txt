H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
Bird flu has been confirmed by The Provincial People's Committee of Nghe An as the cause of an outbreak in Nghe An, Dien Chau District, where nearly 1,200 birds died over the last two weeks. The outbreak occurred at a farm in Dien Hong commune which housed 1,400 chickens and ducks, where some of the birds have tested positive for H5N1, according to the province's Animal Health Department.
