H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
Two brothers from East Kalimantan province have been hospitalized with suspected H5N1 bird flu. They are isolated in the General Hospital Kanudjoso Djatiwibowo. The brothers came in with symptoms such as shortness of breath and have a history of contact with dead chickens.
