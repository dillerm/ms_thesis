H5N1 in Poultry in Taiwan
An outbreak of low pathogenic H5N2 bird flu occurred on a second farm in Chang-Hua prefecture. H5N2 was first identified on January 9 in 20 birds from a farm with 29,000.chickens. After the outbreak was identified, an epidemiological investigation was conducted within a 3 km radius of the farm. A second farm had a positive sample and movement control was implemented on both farms. Samples taken from the originating farm tested negative for the virus on February 5 and movement control was released.
