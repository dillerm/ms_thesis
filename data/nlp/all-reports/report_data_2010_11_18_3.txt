H5N1 in Humans in Hong Kong
A 59-year-old woman in Hong Kong diagnosed with H5N1 bird flu has been quarantined while officials inspect the poultry markets. According to a November 18 statement by Secretary for Food and Health, York Chow, there was no evidence of human-to-human transmission. The patient had recently visited China with her family and ate chicken in a poultry market there, but it is uncertain whether this was the cause of her infection or whether she caught the disease upon her return to Hong Kong on November 1.
