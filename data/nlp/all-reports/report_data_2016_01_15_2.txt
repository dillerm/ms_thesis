France (HPAI H5N1, H5N2)
According to the French Ministry of Agriculture, one case of highly pathogenic avian influenza H5N1 has been reported in poultry in the town of Lafitte-Vigordane, in the Department of Haute-Garonne which is outside of the restriction zone previously established in southwestern France. The farm had 10,000 chickens and 7,000 ducks at the time of detection. A second outbreak was detected in a farm with 1,280 ducks in town of Orin in the Department Pyrenees-Atlantiques. Highly pathogenic avian influenza H5N2 has been detected in the ducks. French officials have instituted sanitary regulations and poultry movement limitations to prevent spread. The source of the outbreaks is unknown.
