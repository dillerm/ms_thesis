Vietnam (HPAI H5N6)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) reports a new outbreak of highly pathogenic H5N6 avian influenza in a flock of backyard poultry in Qu?ng Ngãi Province, central Vietnam.  The outbreak occurred on February 12; 400 birds died and the remaining 2385 were destroyed.  Control measures include vaccination in response to the outbreak, disinfection/disinfestation, stamping out, and surveillance outside containment and/or protection zone.  There has been no treatment of affected animals Avian Influenza in Humans
