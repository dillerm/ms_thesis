South Africa (H7N1 & H7N7)
 The South African veterinary authority and the World Health Organization confirmed on December 24 that three ostrich farms in the Blue Crane Route region of Eastern Cape Province reported cases of H7N1 between October 20 and November 20.  In total, 64 of the 2,906 birds tested positive with the virus.  Additionally, an outbreak of H7N7 was confirmed on an ostrich farm in Oudtshoorn in Western Cape Province in early October.  One bird in a commercial flock of 1,005 tested positive for the virus.  The source of both outbreaks remains unknown.
