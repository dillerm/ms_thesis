Taiwan (HPAI H5N2)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported a case of highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus on a farm in Tainan City.  Samples were tested at the Animal Health Research Institute.  A total of 3,583 chickens died of the disease, and the remaining 4,887 were culled to prevent it spreading further.  Additional control measures implemented are movement control, screening, disinfection, quarantine, stamping out, and zoning.  The cause of this outbreak is unknown.
