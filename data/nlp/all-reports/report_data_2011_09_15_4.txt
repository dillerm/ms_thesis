H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
In the Yogyakarta Province of Indonesia, a 35 year-old-mother and her two children, an 11-year-old and 2.5-year-old, have been under treatment in the bird flu isolation unit of Sardjito Hospital since the beginning of September. After exhibiting symptoms of bird flu, all 3 patients were referred to the hospital, and samples have been sent to the Ministry of Health Laboratory for confirmation. The patients, though improving, remain under treatment and will not be charged a medical fee as bird flu suspect patients.
