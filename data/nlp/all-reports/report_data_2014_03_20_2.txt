Avian Influenza in Poultry in China (HPAI H5N1).
The Chinese veterinary authorities and the World Health Organization confirmed an outbreak of highly pathogenic avian influenza at the junction of three villages in the southern part of Yunnan province. Of 533,000 birds, 29,600 died after contracting the disease while the remaining 503,400 were destroyed to prevent the disease from spreading. Control measures being applied include stamping out, movement control, screening and zoning, and the disinfection of contaminated areas. The source of this outbreak remains unknown.
