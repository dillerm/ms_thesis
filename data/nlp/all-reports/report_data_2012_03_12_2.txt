H5N1 in Poultry in Israel
Two villages in Hadarom district began experiencing an outbreak of H5N1 bird flu on March 7. The villages housed a total of 51,000 turkeys, of which 19,500 fell ill and 10,500 died. Tests were conducted by the Avian Diseases Division of the Kimron Veterinary Institute. The source of the outbreak is unknown, but precautionary measures such as quarantine, movement control, and zoning have been carried out. There are also plans to cull the remaining birds, screen, and disinfect the premises.
