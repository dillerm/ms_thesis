H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
The Ministry of Health has announced a new human case of infection with H5N1 bird flu. A 30-year-old woman from Alexandria governorate developed symptoms on April 26 and was hospitalized on May 3. She received treatment with oseltamivir but died on May 9. Investigations into the source of the illness suggest that the woman had been exposed to sick poultry that may have had bird flu. The Egyptian Central Public Health Laboratory confirmed the case.
