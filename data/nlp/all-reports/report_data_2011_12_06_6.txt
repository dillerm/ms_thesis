H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
A case of H5N1 bird flu has been confirmed in a 31-year-old woman from Dakahlia governorate. She developed symptoms on November 10 and was hospitalized on November 16. She is in critical condition and has been placed on a respirator. An investigation into the source of the infection revealed that the patient had handled sick and dead poultry in her backyard. The Egyptian Central Public Health Laboratories confirmed the disease as H5N1 on November 21. Of the 153 cases confirmed in Egypt to date, 52 have been fatal.
