H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
Bird flu (H5N1) was confirmed as the cause of death for a 19-year-old woman who died on February 13. She had been admitted to a hospital in Tangerang district on the outskirts of the capital Jakarta the day before after having a fever for four days. A health ministry team that conducted surveillance of her house and neighborhood was unable to find out how the woman had contracted the disease. This is the third death from bird flu in Jakarta this year and there have been 150 deaths from the virus reported in Indonesia since 2003.
