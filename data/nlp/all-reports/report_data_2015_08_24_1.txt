China (HPAI H5N6)
The World Organisation for Animal Health Report (OIE) has reported an additional case of the highly pathogenic H5N6 avian influenza virus in the city of Qingyuan in the Guangdong Province.  A total of 1,350 birds died and the remaining 820 birds were culled to prevent further spread of the virus.  In addition to culling, movement control inside the country, screening, disinfection, stamping out and zoning measures were employed. The source of the outbreak is unknown.
