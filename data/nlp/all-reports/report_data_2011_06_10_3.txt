H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
The Ministry of Health announced a confirmed case of human infection with H5N1 bird flu on June 3. A 1-year-old girl from Jakarta province developed symptoms on April 3, was hospitalized on April 8, and has now fully recovered. One week before the onset of the disease, the patient went with her father to the market and handled chickens while there. Laboratory tests confirmed H5N1 bird flu as the cause of illness. Of the 178 cases confirmed to date in Indonesia, 146 have been fatal.
