H7N7 in Poultry in South Africa
Officials from the Department of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries recently reported three outbreaks of low pathogenic H7N7 on commercial ostrich farms in Oudtshoorn and George in the Western Cape Province of South Africa. Out of 9,047 birds, 3,012 were confirmed to have been infected with the virus. Various control measures being applied include quarantine, movement control, and screening.
