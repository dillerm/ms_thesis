H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
A 21-year-old man was admitted to Maamoura Chest Hospital in Sedy Baher District on November 15 with symptoms including fever, cough and difficulty breathing. He received Tamiflu and was reported in stable condition November 17. The man said that he had recently slaughtered sick poultry. According to the Ministry of Health this is the 88th case of H5N1 in Egypt.
