H5N1 in Poultry in South Africa
  Outbreaks of highly pathogenic avian influenza H5N2 virus were reported on three separate farms in South Africa, one in the Eastern Cape Province and two outbreaks in the Western Cape Province.  Out of 1,760 birds, 96 were reported dead and 414 were slaughtered resolving one of the outbreaks.  Two of the outbreaks have not yet been resolved.  The source of the infection remains unknown, and control measures include quarantine and screening.
