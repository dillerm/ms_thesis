H5N1 in Poultry in Egypt
There were four reported outbreaks (one in the Gharbia Governorate and three in the Menoufia Governorate) of highly pathogenic avian influenza in chickens and ducks between January 3 and 9. To prevent the spread of the virus, 67 chickens and 20 ducks were destroyed. The cause of the outbreak is unknown. The birds tested positive for H5 at the National Laboratory for Veterinary Quality Control on Poultry Production (NLQP).
