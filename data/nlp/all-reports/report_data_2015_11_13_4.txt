Avian Influenza in Humans in China (LPAI H7N9)
The National Health and Family Planning Commission has reported two people in the Zhejiang Province have contracted highly pathogenic H7N9 avian influenza virus. The women, a 51 year old farmer and 62 year old with poultry exposure are now in serious condition. Since October there have been four human cases of the virus and since 2013 there have been 661, all of the H7N9 subtype.  To prevent further spread of the disease, the government is engaged in an extensive public health campaign to increase awareness of the disease and measures people can take to protect themselves.
