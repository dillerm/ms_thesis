H5N1 in Poultry in Taiwan
 An outbreak of low pathogenic avian influenza was reported November 10 in the Hsinchu County of Taiwan.  The infection was detected on a native chicken breeder farm during active surveillance, and tests conducted on November 15 at the Animal Health Research Institute confirmed the chickens were infected with the H5N2 strain.  Movement restriction was immediately implemented, while clinical and epidemiological investigation of surrounding poultry farms is in progress.  Additional control measures include quarantine, screening, zoning, and disinfection.  The source of the outbreak remains unknown.
