H5N1 in Poultry in China
No poultry outbreaks were reported in association with the eight human cases this year. However, H5N1 has been detected in dead domestic birds in the northwestern province of Xinjiang. In an outbreak that began on Feb. 1, 519 birds have died in Hetian, and the Chinese Ministry of Agriculture said they detected H5N1 from the samples. A total of 13,218 birds were destroyed
