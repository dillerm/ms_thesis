H5N1 in Poultry in Taiwan
(LPAI). In Changhua County, approximately 7,000 chickens from a farm of 50,000 died of H5N2 bird flu. According to a report, no sick chickens were sold on the market. "The bird flu epidemic in the farm has been under control. All staff and animals have been isolated but we are not going to kill all chickens in the farm," said Chou-Che Kuo, head of the prevention unit of Changhua county.
