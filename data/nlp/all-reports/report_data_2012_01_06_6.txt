H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
 In the Daqahliyah Governorate of Egypt, a 29-year-old man developed bird flu symptoms on December 8 and was admitted to a hospital on December 15 where he was treated with oseltamivir.  The man remained in critical condition and died on December 19.  After investigation, it was found the man had exposure to backyard poultry before he developed symptoms of infection.  Out of 156 cases of humans infected with highly pathogenic avian influenza in Egypt, 54 have proved fatal.
