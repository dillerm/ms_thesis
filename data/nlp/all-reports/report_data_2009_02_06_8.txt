H5N1 in Humans in China
A 21-year-old woman from Huaihua in the central province of Hunan has been confirmed with bird flu, the provincial health bureau said on Jan 31. She fell ill on Jan 23 and was hospitalized on Jan 26. She is said to be stable condition. The woman  had contact with birds that had recently died and some reports suggest that the birds had died from H5N1. If true, this will be the first time in the eight cases in China this year, where poultry has been found to be infected with the virus.
