Indonesia (H5N1)
 The Indonesian Ministry of Health, Directorate General of Disease Control and Environmental Health, recently confirmed that a 28-year-old man from the West Java Province died after contracting the H5N1 virus early in September.  The source of the outbreak remains unknown.  The victim lived near a pigeon racing arena and attended a market in a neighboring town and bought chicken pieces days before he became ill.  Since 2005, Indonesia has reported 194 cases of the H5N1 virus, 162 resulting in death.
