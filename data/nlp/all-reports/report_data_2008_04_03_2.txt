H5N1 in Poultry in South Korea
A bird flu outbreak has been confirmed at a poultry farm in Kimje, 200 km south of Seoul, the Ministry of Food and Agriculture announced on April 2. The farm has over 150,000 birds and nearly 2,400 have died since March 28. The deaths were reported to the ministry on March 31. Samples from the farm tested positive for H5N1 avian influenza. South Korea has had seven outbreaks of H5N1 between November 2006 and March 2007, two of which occurred at another farm in the same city.
