Avian Influenza in Humans in China (LPAI H7N9)
A 58-year-old woman in Jiangsu province who bought and slaughtered a chicken is now in critical condition, suffering from H7N9 avian influenza. The ban on selling live poultry in the cities of Guangzhou, Foshan and Shenzhen will be expanded to the entire province of Guangdong. Imports from mainland China to Hong Kong are being strictly monitored to prevent further spread of the virus.
