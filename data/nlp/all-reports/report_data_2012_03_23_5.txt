H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
The Ministry of Health and Population reported a case of human infection with avian influenza, H5N1, in the Daqahlia governorate on March 19. A 40 year-old female first exhibited symptoms on March 6, and was hospitalized six days later. She received oseltamivir immediately, but died on March 15. The source is believed to be exposure to sick backyard poultry. The case was confirmed by the Central Public Health Laboratories (NIC).
