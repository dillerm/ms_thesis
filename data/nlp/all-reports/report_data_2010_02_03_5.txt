H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
Bird flu is recurring in Dien Bien province. The outbreak began on January 14 and 1,500 chickens have been culled so far. Provincial officials have taken preventative measures such as a ban on the slaughter, transport, and trade of poultry; a ban on processing, eating, or improperly disposing of poultry; and movement control. A second outbreak has been reported in the province of Soc Trang; 350 birds were killed and H5N1 has been confirmed.
