H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
A 44-year-old poultry delivery man from the West Java Province of Indonesia is under treatment in an isolation unit of the Hasan Sadikin hospital for suspected bird flu.  After the patient had contact with dead birds, he developed a runny nose, sore throat, and coughing.  The hospital treated him with Tamiflu and has sent swab samples to the Ministry of Health laboratory for confirmation of bird flu.  The man remains in recovery.
