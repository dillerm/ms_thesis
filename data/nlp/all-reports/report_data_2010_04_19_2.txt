H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
The Ministry of Health announced a case of H5N1 bird flu in an 18-year-old woman from Fayoum governorate. She was admitted to the hospital on March 31 and treated with oseltamivir, but died on April 4. An investigation suggested that exposure to sick and dead poultry was likely the cause of infection. The Egyptian Central Public Health Laboratories confirmed H5N1 as the cause of her death.
