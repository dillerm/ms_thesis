Taiwan (LPAI)
 A poultry farm in Chang-Hua city experienced an outbreak of low pathogenic bird flu starting September 20. Testing done by the Animal Health Research Institute as part of routine surveillance revealed that 20 birds tested at the 18,000-bird farm were positive for H5N2 bird flu. Measures taken to prevent the spread of the disease included movement control, quarantine, screening, zoning, and disinfection of the premises. A clinical and epidemiological investigation of nearby poultry farms and a viral pathogenicity test are underway.
