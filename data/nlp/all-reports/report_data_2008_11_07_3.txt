H5N1 in Poultry in Laos
Bird flu has been detected at a village in the northern province of Xayaboury. The Xayaboury provincial Avian Influenza Control Committee declared October 3rd that all poultry within 1 km of the village will be culled, while villagers who have poultry located within 5 km were told to kill their birds at the first sign of illness without waiting for positive test results for avian influenza.
