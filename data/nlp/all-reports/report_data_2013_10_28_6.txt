Vietnam (H5N1)
 Vietnamese health officials confirmed one new outbreak of the H5N1 virus in the Hoa Binh province on the 14th of October.  Of the 1175 chickens and ducks, 370 died while the remaining 805 were destroyed.  The source of the outbreaks remains unknown and control measures being applied including stamping out, movement control, and disinfection of contaminated areas.
