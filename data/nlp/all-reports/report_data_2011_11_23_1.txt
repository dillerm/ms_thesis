H5N1 in Poultry in Indonesia
 In the South Sulawesi province of Indonesia, outbreaks of H5N1 bird flu have killed approximately 14,000 chickens in three sub-districts.  The first outbreak began in the Watang Sawitto sub-district killing 6,500 chickens, and then spread killing an additional 7,500 chickens in the Tellung Palie of Suppa and Lanrisang sub-districts.  The Livestock Service culled and buried the infected chickens to prevent the spread of the virus.
