China (LPAI H7N9)
The Health and Family Planning Commission of Guangdong Province reported 3 additional cases of low pathogenic H7N9 avian influenza virus in humans.  The cases, reported to the Centre for Health Protection (CHP) on February 1, involve a 60-year-old woman from Chaozhou, a 59-year-old man from Jiangmen, and a 76-year-old woman from Zhaoqing.  There has been no report on their current condition, or how they contracted the disease.
