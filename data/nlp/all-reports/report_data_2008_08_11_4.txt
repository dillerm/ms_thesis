H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
The Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development announced that bird flu has been detected in a farm in Quang Ngai province. 70 ducks died and another 150 fell ill in the outbreak that occurred late last month in July. The ministry said that avian influenza H5N1 was detected in some of the samples. A separate outbreak occurred in Don Thap province where bird flu was found in a flock of 1,000 chickens.
