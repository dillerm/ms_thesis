H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
The Ministry of Health and Population reported that a 42 year-old man from the Menofia Governorate was infected with the H5N1 virus. His symptoms began on December 16. He was admitted to the hospital five days later and received oseltamivir treatment. The man died on December 22. This was the 55th fatality of the 157 cases of human bird flu infection in Egypt.
