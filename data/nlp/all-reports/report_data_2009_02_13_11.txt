H5N1 in Humans in Japan
Panasonic has ordered its overseas staff home from parts of Asia, Africa, Eastern Europe and South America by September 2009 because of concern about a potential pandemic. “This is part of our preparations for a new type of influenza,” said Akira Kadota a spokesman for Panasonic. “We chose areas after considering the prevalence of bird flu, and the capability of medical facilities and access to them.”
