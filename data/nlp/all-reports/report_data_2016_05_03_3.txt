Vietnam (HPAI H5N1)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported an additional case of highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus in a backyard in Can Tho province in the Mekong Delta region of the country.  A total of 140 birds died of the disease, and the remaining 255 were culled to prevent the virus spreading further.  Additional control measures include stamping out, vaccination, disinfection, and surveillance.  The source of this outbreak is unknown.
