H5N1 in Wild birds in Hong Kong
 An Oriental Magpie Robin (Copsychus saularis) found dead on February 7 at Yuen Long tested positive for H5N1 bird flu. The cause of infection is unknown but precautionary measures are being taken such as quarantine, movement control of poultry, screening, zoning, disinfection of the area, and dipping. Tests were performed by Tai Lung Veterinary Laboratory and the Centre of Influenza Research. The cause of the outbreak is unknown.
