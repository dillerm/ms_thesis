Italy (HPAI H5N8)
Highly pathogenic H5N8 avian influenza has been detected in Northern Italy’s Pordenone and Verona provinces. The outbreak of the virus has resulted in the death of 56 birds on two local farms.  The virus outbreak was first seen on January 20in Italy’s Venezia province and resulted in the death of 600 birds and the culling of 19,900 turkeys. Control measure applied include movement control inside the country, screening, traceability, surveillance within containment, zoning, vaccination prohibited, and no treatment of affected animals.
