H5N1 in Poultry in Poland
A new H5N1 outbreak was reported on December 10 in the village of Saldowo, near Biezun in Central Poland, at a broiler farm with 360,000 birds, according to the farm minister Marek Sawicki. Poland is a major exporter of poultry products to the EU, shipping 230,000 metric tons of poultry products in 2006.
