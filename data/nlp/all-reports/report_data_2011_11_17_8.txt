H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
 The Ministry of Egypt notified the World Health Organization that a 1-year-old male from the Mahala district has recovered from the H5N1 virus.  He developed symptoms September 17, was hospitalized September 21, and was discharged from the hospital September 25.  The patient did have contact with poultry before developing symptoms.  Out of 152 human cases of highly pathogenic avian influenza infection in Egypt, 52 have been fatal.
