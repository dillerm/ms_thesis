The mass mortality event described in the Wild Animals section also affected some domestic ducks and chickens in the Masaka District, killing 7 and sickening 20 birds.  "In Masaka alone, wild and domestic birds are dying at alarming numbers, and in Lutembe wetland, white-winged terns are the ones dying," the report to OIE stated.
