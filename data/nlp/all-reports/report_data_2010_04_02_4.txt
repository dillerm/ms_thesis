H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
The Ministry of Health announced on March 28 that a 30-year-old woman from Damietta Governorate has been diagnosed with bird flu. She was admitted to the hospital on March 24, treated with oseltamivir, and is in critical condition. An investigation indicated that the source of the illness is likely exposure to sick and dead poultry. The case was confirmed by the Egyptian Central Public Health Laboratories.
