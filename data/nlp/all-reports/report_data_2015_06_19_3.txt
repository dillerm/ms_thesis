Iran (HPAI H5N1)
An additional case of the highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus has been reported in a backyard flock of 25 birds in a village in Amol, Mazandaran Province.  17 of the birds died and the rest were called to prevent further spread of the virus.  In addition to culling, an investigation is underway to trace the source of the outbreak and active surveillance is ongoing.
