H5N1 in Poultry in Nepal
A poultry farm in Kathmandu reported the death of roughly 15,160 chickens on March 13. The Animal Health Directorate confirmed the cause of the die-offs, which began on March 2, was H5N1. The chickens and feed originated from the Narayangadh-based Abinash hatchery. Control measures have included culling, disinfecting, and dumping the feed.
