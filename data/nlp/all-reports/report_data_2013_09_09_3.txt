Avian Influenza in Poultry in Mexico
 Mexican officials confirmed two outbreaks of H7N3 earlier this week.  The first outbreak, at a backyard farm in the state of Guanajuarto, reported that five quails were affected and that the situation has been resolved.  The second outbreak, in the state of Jalisco, was confirmed at a layers farm with 10,000 birds.  Information is still coming in about this outbreak and the number of deaths is not yet known.  Control measures being applied including vaccination, screening and zoning, and movement control throughout the country.
