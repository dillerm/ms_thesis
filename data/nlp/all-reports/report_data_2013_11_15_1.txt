Nepal (HPAI H5N1)
 Nepalese officials confirmed that 85 new cases of H5N1 have occurred since August in three zones (Bagmati, Narayani, and Bheri).  43,691 birds have died of infection and 1,372309 were culled to prevent the disease from spreading further.  Intensive surveillance activities have been implemented throughout the country and control measures being applied include disinfection of contaminated farms, movement control throughout the country, and control of wildlife reservoirs.
