H5N1 in Poultry in Nepal
Officials from the Nepalese Department of Livestock Services confirmed three outbreaks of the highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus earlier this week. In the Narayani and Lumbini Zones, out of 1,675 birds, 410 died while 1,165 were destroyed to prevent further spread of the virus. The source of these outbreaks remains unknown. Various control measures being applied include stamping out, movement control, quarantine, and disinfection of contaminated areas.
