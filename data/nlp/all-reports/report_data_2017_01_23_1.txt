India (HPAI H5N1)
Highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza has been confirmed in samples from 53 crows which were found dead in the city of Bhubaneswar, Odisha State, in the first two weeks of January.  To date, no domestic chickens from the area have been affected, and there are no restrictions on poultry consumption.  Chickens are being vaccinated against HPAI H5N1 as a precaution, and any carcasses of crows and cranes are to be burned.
