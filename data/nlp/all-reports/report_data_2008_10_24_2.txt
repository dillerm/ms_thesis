H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh
H5N1 has been found in Naogaon district, the first appearance of the virus in three months. A spokesman from the Livestock Department, Salahuddin Khan, said that more than 300 birds have been destroyed at a farm following the discovery of H5N1 there last week. According to press reports, the districts of Habigunj, Srimanagal and Sylhet, which border India, have also recently been affected by avian influenza.  No additional information is available on these outbreaks. Bangladesh has been battling bird flu since February 2007.
