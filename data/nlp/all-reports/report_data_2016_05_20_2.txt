Ghana (HPAI H5N1)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported 4 cases of highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus at farms in the Centra and Eastern provinces, and the Greater Accra Regions.  A total of 1,278 birds died from the disease, and the remaining 7,722 were culled to prevent it spreading further.  Additional control measures include: movement control; disinfection; quarantine; and stamping out.  The source of this outbreak is unknown.
