H5N1 in poultry. in Hong Kong
 Two new cases of H5N1 bird flu in chickens were reported to OIE this week, both involving badly decomposed carcasses.  The first case was one carcass collected on January 25; then two chicken carcasses were collected on January 28. All three chickens were collected from the Lantau Island district at the shore of Tai O from unknown sources. The origins of the infections are currently unknown.
