H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia.
Indonesian Health Minister Siti Fadilah Supari said on June 5 that the government will no longer announce human deaths due to bird flu immediately upon confirmation . The deaths will be reported as a group at intervals. The policy will lead to significant delay in the recognition of new human cases globally since the World Health Organization does not have an independent means of verification; instead it relies on the in-country declaration of confirmed cases in Indonesia. The 108 fatal cases in Indonesia account for almost half of the 241 recorded globally since 2003. (more...)
