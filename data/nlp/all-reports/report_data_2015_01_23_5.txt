Japan (HPAI H5N8)
According to a World Organisation for Animal Health report, the highly pathogenic H5N8 avian influenza virus was confirmed through gene sequencing at a broiler breeder farm in Aryita in Saga prefecture on the island of Kyushu.  Eight birds died, and around 72,900 have been culled.  Movement restrictions have been placed on farms within a 3-kilometer radius, and shipping is restricted from farms within a 10-kilometer radius.
