Hong Kong ex China (LPAI H7N9)
A 68-year-old woman is in critical condition with H7N9 bird flu in Tuen Mun Hospital. She contracted the virus in while travelling to Longgang district in Shenzhen on Dec 13, where she visited a wet market to purchase vegetables but did not have direct poultry contact. While in Shenzhen, she ate chicken at a home-cooked meal and returned to Hong Kong on the same day. She fell ill on 12/19 and saw doctors at a clinic on 12/19 and 12/23 and was hospitalized on 12/25. Family members, clinic staff and fellow travelers are being monitored for possible infection from contact with the patient.
