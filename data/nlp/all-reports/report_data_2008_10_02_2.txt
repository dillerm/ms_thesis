H5N1 in Poultry in Vietnam
More than 50 ducks died from H5N1 on a farm of 500 birds in Ca Mou province on September 22nd. The Animal Health Department said that all of the ducks on the farm were under 45 days old and had not been vaccinated. Vietnam most recently experienced a series of outbreaks in the southern province of Ben Tre near Ho Chi Minh City. A total of 26 provinces have been affected in 2008.
