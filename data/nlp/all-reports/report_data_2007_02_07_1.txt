H5N1 in Wild Birds in Hong Kong:
A peregrine falcon (Falco peregrinus) found on  January 25, 2007, in Tsuen Wan in the New Territories in Hong Kong has tested  positive for H5N1.  A house crow (Corvus splendens) picked up on  January 22 in Sham Shui Po has also been confirmed to be positive for H5N1.   A total of seven wild birds have tested positive for H5N1 this year in  Hong Kong.
