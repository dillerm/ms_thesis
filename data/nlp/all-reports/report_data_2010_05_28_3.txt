H5N1 in Poultry in Indonesia
During March the Participatory Disease Surveillance and Response (PDSR) program conducted surveillance in 1,984 villages in 27 provinces. They found 1.5 villages newly infected with H5N1 per 1000 villages in the coverage area, but incidence varied greatly by province. The 4 provinces with the greatest number of infected villages were Kepulauan Bangka Belitung, Yogyakarta, Lampung, and Jambi.
