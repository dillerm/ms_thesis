H5N1 in Poultry in Taiwan
The World Organization for Animal Health (OIE) recently reported that an outbreak of highly pathogenic avian influenza (HPAI) H5N2 has been confirmed at a farm in Ma-Gong City, located in the Eastern Taiwan Region. All 800 chickens at the infected poultry farm were culled and poultry farms surrounding the infected area were being tested to prevent further outbreaks.
