Nigeria (HPAI H5N1)
According to the World Organisation for Animal Health Report (OIE), three new cases of highly pathogenic H5N1 have been reported in the state of Lagos and a fourth case in the state of Rivers.  A total of 668 birds died and the remaining 2,932 birds were culled to prevent further spread of the disease.  In addition to culling, disinfection, quarantine and modified stamping out measures were employed along with movement controls inside the country. The source of the outbreak is unknown. Poor farm biosecurity was noted.
