H5N1 in Poultry in Egypt
A total of 34 cases of H5N1 bird flu were reported in 14 governorates between April 1 and May 15 (Behaira 1, Beni-Suef 1, Dakahlia 1, Fayoum 1, Gharbia 1, Helwan 1, Kafr-el-sheikh 5, Luxor 2, Menia 2, Menofia 7, Port Said 1, Qalioubiya 6, Sixth of October 3, and Suez 1). There were 8 commercial farms infected and 6 of them had been vaccinated against bird flu.
