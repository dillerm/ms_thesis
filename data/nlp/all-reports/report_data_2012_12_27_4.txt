Germany (LPAI H5).
Low pathogenic bird flu was reported in the Schleswig-Holstein state on December 21. The outbreak began on a farm of 1522 birds on December 18. The disease was confirmed by the Friedrich-Loffler Institute on December 21 and was identified as the H5 subtype. All of the birds were culled to prevent the virus from spreading. The source of the outbreak is unknown, but control measures such as control of wildlife reservoirs, quarantine, movement control, screening, zoning, and disinfection of the premises have been undertaken. The last outbreak of bird flu in Germany had occurred in August 2011.
