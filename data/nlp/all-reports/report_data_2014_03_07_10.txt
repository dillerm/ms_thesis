Avian Influenza in Poultry in Nepal (HPAI H5N1).
Nepalese health officials confirmed outbreaks of H5N1 at two poultry farms in the city of Itahari, in the Sunsari District in the Koshi Zone. All 1,552 chickens and 20 crates of eggs were destroyed to prevent the disease from spreading. No human cases have been identified in the country at this time.
