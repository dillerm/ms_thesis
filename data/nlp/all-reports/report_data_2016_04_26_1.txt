Egypt (HPAI H5N1)
The Central Department for Preventive Medicine of the General Authority for Veterinary Services, Ministry of Agriculture issued a report on April 18 that said a total of 73 HPAI H5N1 outbreaks have been detected in 16 governorates in Egypt this year. 46 farms, 21 markets and 6 backyard flocks have been affected thus far. They followed a program to dispose of dead birds by burning or burial, cleaning affected areas, and conducting immunization procedures for the center of the infection and villages within a 9 km radius of the infected sites.Â  The cause of the outbreaks is unknown.
