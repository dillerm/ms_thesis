Other Avian Influenza Strains in Poultry in The Netherlands
 Recently, 10 new cases of low pathogenic avian influenza (H7N1) virus in a free-range layer farm in Schore, Zeeland, were reported to OIE. At the time of the report, all of the birds were still alive.  The source of the outbreak is unknown, but measures have been put in place to control the spread of the virus, including stamping, zoning affected areas, and limiting movement of animals within the country.
