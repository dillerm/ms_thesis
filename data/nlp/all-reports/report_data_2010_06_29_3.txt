H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
A 4-year-old boy from West Kalimantan province was hospitalized at Soedarso regional hospital in Pontianak with suspected bird flu. According to the Head of Health Service of Pontianak, Multi Juto Batharendo, “[The] patient is under isolation. Throat swab has been sent to Laboratory of Health Research and Development (Litbangkes). Epidemiology investigation didn’t show other similar case.” Many birds have died recently in the area and Soedarso regional hospital is the province’s bird flu referral hospital, so they have been preparing their facilities in anticipation of bird flu patients. In the meantime, the Health Service has enacted preventive measures such as epidemiological investigation, public socialization and distribution of Tamiflu to public health centers.
