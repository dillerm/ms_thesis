Up to now, Beijing reported three cases of human infection with H7N9 influenza in 2016. The man has a history of live poultry exposure. In the Henan province, the Family Planning Committee reported its first case of H7N9 avian influenza in humans. The patient is a 54-year-old female farmer whose exposure to poultry or live bird markets is unknown. This is the first case in Henan since 2013.
