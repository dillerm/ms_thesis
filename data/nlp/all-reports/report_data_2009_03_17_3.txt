H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh
The Department of Fisheries and Livestock detected bird flu virus and worked to cull birds at various locations around the country. Five outbreaks in three provinces (Chittagong, Dhaka and Rajshahi) were reported to the OIE (World Organization for Animal Health) this week. 2,095 chickens and 205 eggs were destroyed in Netrakona and Gaibandha as of  March 6.
