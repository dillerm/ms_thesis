H7N9 in Humans in China
 Chinese authorities confirmed that between April 24th and May 1st, 19 people became infected with the H7N9 virus and a 55-year-old man died on mainland China, bringing the total of infected people up to 127.  Of the 19 new cases, 16 originated the eastern provinces of Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Jiangxi, Fujian, and Shandong; while the other 3 were found in the central provinces of Hunan and Henan.  To date, the H7N9 virus has killed 27 people in mainland China while an additional 26 have made a full recovery after being infected.
