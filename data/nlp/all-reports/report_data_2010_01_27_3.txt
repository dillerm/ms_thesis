H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh
An outbreak of H5N1 bird flu occurred in 250 birds from a farm of 1,182 in Rajshahi division on January 4. The rest of the flock was culled and the premises were disinfected to prevent the spread of the disease. Samples tested positive for H5N1 bird flu on January 17 at the Bangladesh Livestock Research Institute. The origin of the outbreak is unknown.
