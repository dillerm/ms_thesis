Vietnam (HPAI H5N6)
An outbreak of highly pathogenic H5N6 avian influenza was reported in Quang Ngai Province, southern Vietnam, on 7 March.  The outbreak began on 2 March at a poultry farm.  Authorities have culled over 4,000 chickens in response to the outbreak.  This is the first report of this strain in Vietnam.
