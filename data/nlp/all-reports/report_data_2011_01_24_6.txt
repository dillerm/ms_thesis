H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
On January 19, Dr. Abdel Rahman Shahin, spokesman for the Egyptian Ministry of Health, reported that a new human case of H5N1 bird flu has been discovered. An 18-month-old child in Alexandria governorate was admitted to a hospital and is suffering from high fever, cough, and febrile convulsion. The child was given Tamiflu and is now in stable condition.
