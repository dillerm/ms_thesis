H5N1 in Poultry in Netherlands
A poultry farm in Limburg province culled 42,700 turkeys after on outbreak with 200 cases of low pathogenic avian influenza (LPAI) H5N2 was confirmed on March 17. All birds were destroyed on March 19. The source of the outbreak is unknown. Control measures already applied include stamping out, movement control inside the country, screening, zoning, and disinfection of infected premises. The LPAI test results came from the Central Veterinary Institute, a national laboratory in Lelystad.
