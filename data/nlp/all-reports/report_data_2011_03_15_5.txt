H5N1 in poultry. in Japan
  About 33,000 broiler chickens were culled on March 6 at a poultry farm in the town of Kadogawa, Miyazaki Prefecture.  Prefectural officials said the culling occurred after the 13th case of H5N1 bird flu in the prefecture was confirmed the previous day. Since January 22, 13 cases of bird flu have been confirmed at poultry farms in Miyazaki Prefecture and about 990,000 chickens have been slaughtered.
