H5N1 in Poultry in Pakistan
A commercial farm in the Mansehra District of Pakistan lost 22,607 chickens out of a total flock of 60,000 on a disease outbreak that began on July 21, acording to a report to the World Organization for Animal Health (OIE). Rueters and the Center for Infectious Disease Research and Policy reported on August 21 that HPAI H5N1 has been confirmed.
