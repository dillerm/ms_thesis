H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
A 17-year-old girl from Riau province was treated at Husada hospital for suspected bird flu based on a medical examination. Chickens have died in the neighborhood and have tested positive for bird flu according to local reports.  As of October 1, the patient had returned home and was being treated as an outpatient. A 15-year-old boy from East Kalimantan province was isolated in Kanujoso Djatiwibowo hospital in Balikpapan on October 3. The patient was admitted with a high fever on October 2 after chickens that were raised in his house died suddenly. The chief of the neighborhood has instructed residents to cull all chickens in the area to stop the spread of the disease in case the chickens died from bird flu.
