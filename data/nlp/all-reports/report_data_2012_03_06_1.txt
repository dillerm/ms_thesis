H5N1 in Wild birds in Hong Kong
It was reported on February 29 that a dead oriental magpie robin, the second found in Cheung Chau within two days, tested positive for H5N1 bird flu in preliminary checks. They had announced on February 28 that a crested goshawk found dead in Mong Kok tested positive for H51 bird flu. The department has stepped up disinfection and cleaning in these areas.
