France (HPAI H5N8)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported a new case of highly pathogenic H5N8 avian influenza virus in the department of Haute-Savoie. A wild gull was found dead at Evian onÂ  November 25, and the disease was confirmed after testing by the National Reference Laboratory. Farms in the area will be contained, have extra biosecurity enforced, and will undergo a veterinary check-up. Additional control measures include screening, disinfection and disinfestation, stamping out, and official disposal of carcasses, by-products, and waste. The source of this outbreak has been stated as contact with wild species.
