Taiwan (HPAI H5N2)
The Council of Agriculture (COA) has reported the presence of highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus at a chicken farm in Pingtung County.  The 3,088 chickens present at the farm were culled to prevent further spread of the disease.  In accordance with standard procedures, the Bureau of Animal and Plant Health Inspection and Quarantine also cleaned and disinfected the area.  The source of this outbreak was not reported.
