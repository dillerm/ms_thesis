H5N1 in Poultry in India
In the Indian state of Tripura last month, an outbreak of H5N1 bird flu was reported in a district poultry farm. Out of a total of 4,668 birds there were 3,168 deaths. The source of the outbreak remains unknown. Control measures applied include: quarantine, disinfection of the affected area, movement control, and ongoing surveillance within a 10 km radius of the farm to prevent the spread of the disease.
