H5N1 in Poultry in Indonesia
The Participatory Disease Surveillance & Response (PDSR) programme covers 71,033 villages in 84% of Indonesiaâ€™s 448 districts and municipalities in 29 of its 33 provinces, using a network of 33 Local Disease Control Centres. During May 2010, PDSR conducted surveillance in 1,978 villages (2.8%). The overall H5N1 bird flu incidence was 0.6 infected villages per 1,000 villages under surveillance. The four provinces with the highest incidence values were Yogyakarta, Sumatera Barat, Kalimantan Timur, and Lampung. Some newly infected villages were found during May with 14 in Sumatra, 29 in Java, and 2 in Kalimantan.
