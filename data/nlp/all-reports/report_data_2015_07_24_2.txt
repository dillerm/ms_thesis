Avian Influenza in Poultry in Taiwan (HPAI H5N2)
Two new cases of the highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus have been reported at a duck farm in Taixi Township, Yunlin County and at a poultry farm in Changzhi Township, Pingtung County.  1,540 birds died and the remaining 7,536 were culled to prevent further spread of the disease.  The affected premises have also been quarantined and surrounding farms within a 3 km radius have been placed under intensified surveillance.
