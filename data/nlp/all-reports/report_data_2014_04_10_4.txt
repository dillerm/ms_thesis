Avian Influenza in Humans in China (LPAI H7N9).
Chinese health officials confirmed that three people are in critical condition and two are dead after contracting the H7N9 virus. The victims in critical condition range in age from 28-72 and are from the provinces Jiangsu, Guangdong, and Fujian. Details about the two deceased victims were not provided. So far this year, China has reported more than 273 human H7N9 cases, including at least 36 deaths.
