South Africa (HPAI H5N2)
The World Organisation for Animal Health Report (OIE) has reported two more cases of the highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus on commercial ostrich farms in the Western Cape Province.  A total 343 ostriches were infected, no deaths were reported and no birds were culled.  To prevent further spread of the disease, quarantine, screening and movement control are being employed6
