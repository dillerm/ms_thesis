H5N1 in Poultry in Germany
Low pathogenic avian influenza H5N3 has been found in two locations in Leipzig. Following detection of the virus, 106 birds on a farm have been and a goose and three ducks from the Leipzig Zoo have also been culled.  None of the birds exhibited any clinical symptoms.  Highly pathogenic avian influenza was found in Goerlitz, 200 km to the east, resulting in 1,434 birds being culled and the creation of a quarantine zone.
