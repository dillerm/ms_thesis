LPAI H7N9 in Poultry in China
 According to the China Animal Disease Control, a total of four outbreaks of the low pathogenic avian H7N9 influenza virus have been confirmed throughout China.  At a farm in the Jiangsu province, there was one confirmed case and all 340 remaining birds were culled.  Three outbreaks were also reported at open markets in the Henan and Zhejiang provinces.  Two of the three infected birds were culled.  In all four cases, the source of these outbreaks remains unknown and control measures being applied include screening, zoning, disinfection of contaminated areas, control of wildlife reservoirs, and movement control around the country.
