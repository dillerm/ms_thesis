Germany (LPAI)
Low pathogenic H5N2 bird flu was confirmed on a farm in Mecklenburg-Vorpommern. The outbreak was first observed on November 10, confirmed on November 11, and reported on November 15. All 17,000 birds are thought to be susceptible to the disease. No birds have died from the virus and none have been culled. Contact with wild species has been identified as the origin of the outbreak.
