Avian Influenza in Poultry in South Africa (H5N2/H7N1)
Subclinical cases of the low pathogenic H5N2 avian flu virus have been found in commercial ostriches on three farms in Western Cape Province between July 22 and September 1. The veterinary authority sent a follow up report on October 7 to the World Organization for Animal Health (OIE). A total of 1,647 of the 4,099 birds tested positive for the virus. None have died or been destroyed.
