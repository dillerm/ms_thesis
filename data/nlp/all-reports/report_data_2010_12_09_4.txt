H5N1 in Poultry in Nepal
A farm housing 11,503 poultry in Narayani zone experienced an outbreak of H5N1 bird flu beginning on October 25. The disease killed 66 birds on the farm;the rest were culled to prevent the spread of the disease. Other measures such as quarantine, movement control, disinfection of the premises, and dipping/spraying have been enacted. The cause of illness was determined as H5N1 by the Central Veterinary Laboratory on October 27 and this result was confirmed by the Veterinary Laboratories Agency on November 19. The origin of the outbreak is unknown but the event was resolved on October 30.
