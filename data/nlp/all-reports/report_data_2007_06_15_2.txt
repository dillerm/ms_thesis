H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh
H5N1 has spread into two new areas in the Rajshahi Division  in Bangladesh. Approximately 3000 birds have been culled in Dinajpur District,  and 5000 in Rangpur District, following identification of H5N1 in dead birds in  both areas. The districts are located approximately 450 kms northwest of the  capital Dhaka.
