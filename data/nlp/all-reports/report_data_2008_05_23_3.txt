H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
Two teenagers from the same family in South Jakarta have died less than 10 days apart, sparking fears of bird flu. Ahmad Rizki, 15, died on May 4 and Istiqomah, 16, died on May 14. Both had similar symptoms and lived where a neighbor kept chickens. But a survey of their neighborhood in South Jakarta did not detect H5N1 in poultry in the area. Istiqomah was confirmed to have bird flu on May 16, but his brother Ahmad Rizki is said to have typhoid fever, not bird flu. Another family member, Alamsyah, 24, has bird flu-like symptoms and is currently being treated. (more...)
