Nigeria (HPAI H5N1)
Four additional cases of the highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus have been confirmed at three commercial poultry farms in the states of Abia, Enugu, and Lagos, and at a backyard flock in Lagos.  3,417 birds died, and the remaining 2,089 were destroyed to prevent further spread of the disease. Some of the birds had been purchased from an infected state, although the exact source of the outbreak is unknown.
