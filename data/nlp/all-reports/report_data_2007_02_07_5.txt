H5N1 in Domestic Animals in Indonesia:
Two pigs in Bali have been found to be infected with  “bird flu.”  Zhong Nanshan, speaking at a conference in Guangzhou, said  the pigs generated "a variant of the bird flu virus after they contracted  bird flu."  No further details are known at this time as to the  characterization of the viruses, and the implications of the mutations for  either animal or human infection are unknown at present.
