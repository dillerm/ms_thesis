H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
A 33-year-old woman from the Kafr el-Sheikh province, hospitalized on April 15 with a fever and respiratory problems, died from H5N1 bird flu. On April 22 another case of bird flu was reported in a 34-year-old woman from Tanta. She had been in close contact with infected poultry, but she was treated with Tamiflu and is now in stable condition. There have been 26 deaths and 68 human cases of bird flu in Egypt since the disease was first reported.
