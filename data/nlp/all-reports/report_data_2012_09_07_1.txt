H5N1 in Poultry in China
 In the Guangdong province of China, the Guangdong Provincial Agriculture Department is investigating a duck market in the city of Guangzhou after a two-year old boy from Hong Kong visited the market in mid-May and was confirmed to have H5N1 on June 5th.  The Guangzhou Agriculture and Animal Husbandry Department has launched an inspection of the Jiangnandong Market, and according to the Municipal Animal Epidemic Disease Control and Prevention Center, one duck, out of 40 samples, has tested positive for the H5N1 virus.  As of Tuesday, no other confirmed reports of human infection have been reported.
