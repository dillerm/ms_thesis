H7N9 in Humans - Updates in Beijing
 Health authorities in Beijing confirmed earlier this week the second H7N9 human infection of the year in the Municipality.  The victim, a six-year old boy from the Haidian district, presented to the hospital with a fever and a headache and was confirmed to have the virus on Tuesday.  After two days of observation, his temperature returned back to normal and he was back at school the following day.  The boy continues to be in good health.
