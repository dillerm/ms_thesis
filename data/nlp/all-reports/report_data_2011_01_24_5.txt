H5N1 in Poultry in South Korea
From January 6 to 14 there have been disease outbreaks on 23 farms in South Korea. Four of the farms are located in Gyeonggi-do province, eighteen in South Jeolla province, and one in Chungcheognam province. There have been a total of 20,307 cases out of 702,820 susceptible birds and 20,297 have died. The remaining birds have been culled to prevent the spread of the disease. Farmers notified authorities of suspected cases and samples tested at the National Veterinary Research and Quarantine Service were positive for H5N1 bird flu.
