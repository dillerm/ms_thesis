Germany (H5N2)
According to a World Organisation for Animal Health report,  low pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza was discovered on a farm in Bonn, Germany.  Of the 1,732 susceptible birds, 1 died and the rest were destroyed.  No poultry or poultry products from the farm were  moved to other regions of Germany or other countries.  Other control measures taken include screening, zoning, and disinfection of affected premises.  The source of the outbreak is unknown.
