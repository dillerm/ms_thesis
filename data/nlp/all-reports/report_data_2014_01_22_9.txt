Avian Influenza in Humans in Vietnam (HPAI H5N1)
The Vietnamese Department of Preventive Medicine under the Ministry of Health confirmed the country’s first human case of H5N1 of the year on January 20. The victim, a 52-year-old man from southern Binh Phuc province, was hospitalized on January 11 for flu-like symptoms and died on January 18. This is the country’s first case of H5N1 in nine months.
