H5N1 in Poultry in Nepal
According to the Nepalese Department of Livestock Services, a total of 9 outbreaks of H5N1 have been confirmed in three zones throughout Nepal: Lumbini, Mechi, and Narayani. Out of 10,901 birds, 3,982 died while the remaining 6,919 were culled to prevent further spread of the virus. The source of these outbreaks remains unknown. Various control measures being applied include quarantine, movement control, screening, and disinfection of contaminated areas.
