Avian Influenza in Poultry in Libya (HPAI H5N1).
On March 12, health officials confirmed Libya's first outbreak of highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza. The outbreak occurred at a farm in the port city of Tubruq where 35 chickens and ducks were destroyed after 15 were found dead after contracting the virus. Various control measures have been executed to prevent the disease from spreading including stamping out, movement control, and sterilization.
