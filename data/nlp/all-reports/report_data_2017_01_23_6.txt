North Korea (HPAI)
Highly pathogenic avian influenza has been detected on a farm in Sunchon, South Pyongan Province, in central North Korea.  The North Korean government has responded by tightening hygiene and security measures around the ranch producing food for Kim Jong Un, banning the sale of chicken meat in public markets, and educating the public.  Official advice included suggesting that people to gargle with salt water and drink garlic juice.  Despite these measures, meat from infected or culled birds is still being sold on the black market.
