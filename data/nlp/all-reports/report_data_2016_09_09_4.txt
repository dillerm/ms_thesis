South Korea (HPAI H5N8)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported that the stamping-out and disinfection of the last highly pathogenic H5N8 avian influenza virus affected farm has been completed.  In April total of 49 ducks and chickens were culled on the farm in Gyeonggi-do Province.  This was in accordance to the control measures implemented in March, which included: movement control; disinfection and disinfestation; quarantine; stamping out; zoing; and control of wildlife reservoirs.  There has been no recurrence of this disease in South Korea.
