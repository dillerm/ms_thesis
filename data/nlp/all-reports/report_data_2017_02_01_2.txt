Croatia (HPAI H5N1)
The World Organization for Animal Health (OIE) reported an outbreak of highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus in the village Muzilovcica in Sisacko-Moslavacka county in central Croatia. A total of 3 mute swans were found dead beginning on Dec 29, 2016.
