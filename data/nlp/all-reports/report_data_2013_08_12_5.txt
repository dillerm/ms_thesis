Taiwan (H5N3) (This is an adjacent farm)
Taiwanese officials recently reported one outbreak of H5N3 on a duck farm located in Hualien County. This farm is located near the H5N2-affected premise. Out of 13,400 birds, 20 cases were confirmed. Preventative measures applied included quarantine, movement control, screening, disinfection of premises, and no vaccination or treatment of affected animals. The status of this outbreak is considered ongoing.
