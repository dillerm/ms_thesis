H5N1 in Wild birds in Japan
Nagasaki prefecture said February 5 that it has detected H5N1 bird flu virus from a mandarin duck that was found dead at a pond in the city of Nagasaki together with two other ducks on February 4. Another duck found dead in the same pond on January 31 had also tested positive for bird flu. The National Institute for Environmental Studies is conducting a close examination on the sample.
