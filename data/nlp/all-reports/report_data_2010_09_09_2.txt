H5N1 in Poultry in Indonesia
Starting on August 27, many chickens in a village in Aceh province died suddenly. Further investigation by Participatory Disease Surveillance and Response (PDSR), Agriculture of Livestock Service West Aceh has confirmed that those chickens died from H5N1 bird flu. The dead chickens have been burned and buried and the remaining chickens have been culled to prevent the spread of the disease.
