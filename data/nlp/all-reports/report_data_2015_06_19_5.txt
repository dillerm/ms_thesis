Taiwan (HPAI H5N2)
According to a World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) report, three new cases of the highly pathogenic H5N2 avian influenza virus have been reported at farms in Yunlin County.  A total of 8,894 chicken and geese died, and the remaining 14,364 were culled to prevent further spread of the disease.  In addition to culling, the affected farms have been placed under movement restriction and surroundings farm within a 3 km radius are under intensified surveillance.
