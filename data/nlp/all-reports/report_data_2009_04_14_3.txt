H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
The Egyptian news agency MENA reports that a six-year-old boy from Qalyubia contracted the highly pathogenic strain of bird flu. This is the sixth case of human infection with bird flu in Egypt in the space of a month and the 63rd confirmed case of H5N1 since 2003. Health Ministry official Abdel Rahman Shahine says that the boy began to experience symptoms two weeks ago and was admitted to a hospital one week later. He is being treated with tamiflu and is in critical condition.
