Ghana (HPAI H5N1)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) has reported additional cases of highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus in the Central Region and Greater Accra Region.  A total of 1,442 birds died, and the remaining 936 were culled to prevent the disease spreading further.  Additional control measures include movement control, disinfection and disinfestation, quarantine, and stamping out.  The source of this outbreak is unknown.
