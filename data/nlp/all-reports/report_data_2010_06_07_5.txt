H5N1 in Humans in Indonesia
A patient is being treated for suspected bird flu at Soeradji Tirtonegoro hospital in Central Java. The 13-year-old patient was admitted to the hospital on May 26 and placed in isolation because the patient had direct contact with birds that tested positive for the disease. A blood sample was sent to University Laboratory and Ministry of Health and the patient was treated with Tamiflu and infusion support.
