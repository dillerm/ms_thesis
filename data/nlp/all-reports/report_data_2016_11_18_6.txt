The World Organization for Animal Health (OIE) reports recurrences of highly pathogenic avian influenza virus (H5N8) as evidenced in 22 new outbreaks in Bern, Fribourg, Neuchatel, Schaffausen, Thurgau, Valais, and Vaud between November 5 and November 9. In Lake Constance, 12 tufted ducks, two unidentified ducks, and one great crested grebe were found dead.
