H5N1 in Poultry in Indonesia
 The Livestock Service in Central Sulawesi province confirmed that the recent sudden death of chickens was from H5N1 bird flu. The virus was found in samples from dead chickens from several sub-districts.  Examination was conducted in field and confirmed by the veterinary disease investigation center in Maros. Livestock service officers disinfected the infected cages and those in the surrounding area to control the spread of the disease.
