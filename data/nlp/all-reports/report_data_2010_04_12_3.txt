H5N1 in Humans in Vietnam
The National Tropical Diseases Hospital in Hanoi announced on April 5 that a 22-year-old man from Bac Kan province is being treated for bird flu. The man is in serious condition and requires the use of a breathing machine. On April 3, the patient was transferred from the province’s General Hospital with a cough and high fever. Tests run on April 4 by the National Epidemiology Institute were positive for H5N1 bird flu. An investigation found dead waterfowl in the man’s neighborhood, and other people in the area with bird-flu like symptoms have been quarantined. Another case of bird flu in a two-year-old girl from Bac Kan province was announced by the Health Ministry on April 9. No further information is available at this time.
