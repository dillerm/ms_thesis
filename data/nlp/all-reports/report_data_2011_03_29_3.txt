H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh
 It was reported on March 23 that nearly 30,000 chickens were culled and 50,000 eggs were destroyed at a poultry farm in Gazipur after the detection of H5N1 bird flu infection. Sripur Upazila livestock officer A K M Ataur Rahman said that the chickens started dying on March 20 and that samples of the carcasses were sent to Dhaka immediately.  Culling began March 22 and was still occurring at the time of the report.
