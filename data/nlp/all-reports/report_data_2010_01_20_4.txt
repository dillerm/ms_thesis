H5N1 in Humans in Egypt
A 20-year-old woman from Beni Suef governorate was hospitalized for suspected bird flu. The woman had handled dead birds that are thought to have died from the disease. She came down with symptoms such as fever, cough, sore throat and difficulty breathing on January 6 and was hospitalized on January 11. She was treated with Tamiflu and is in stable condition. This is the 91st case of bird flu in Egypt since the first outbreak of the disease.
