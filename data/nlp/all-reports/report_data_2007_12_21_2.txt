H5N1 in Poultry in Bangladesh
Bangladesh has reported 35 H5N1 outbreaks in poultry  to the World Organization for Animal Health (OIE) that occurred from March 26 to December 12, 2007. On December 17,  two H5N1 outbreaks were reported in the Galbanda District. In one, 57 birds died from a flock of 622 in an outbreak that began on December 5; in the other, 8 birds died from a flock of 476 in an outbreak that began on December 10.
