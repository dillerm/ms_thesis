Bhutan (HPAI H5N1)
The World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) reports a case of highly pathogenic H5N1 avian influenza virus on farms and backyards in Alubari, Wangkha village in the Chukha District beginning on October 13, 2016. A total of 13 chickens died of the disease, and 61 were destroyed out of a susceptible population of 3,000. Additional control measures include: quarantine; movement control; disinfection and disinfestation; surveillance; dipping and spraying; and modified stamping out.
