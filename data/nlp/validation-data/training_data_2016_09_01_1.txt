USA (HPAI H5N2)
An apparently healthy mallard, sampled as part of a national surveillance program to detect highly pathogenic avian influenza has been confirmed by the USDA to have been infected with the same HPAI H5N2 virus that affected poultry farms in the Midwest in 2014/2015. The bird was among a group of waterfowl sampled at a wildlife refuge located outside Fairbanks, Alaska. This is the first appearance of the H5N2 virus since June 2015, and the first time any HPAI viruses have been reported in Alaska. Avian Influenza in Poultry
