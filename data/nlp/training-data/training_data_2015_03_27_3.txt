USA (HPAI H5N2)
Highly pathogenic avian influenza H5N2 has been detected in a Canada goose in Montana. A sick Canada goose was collected near Cheyenne, MT had testing has now confirmed the bird was infected with the same strain of H5N2 that has been spreading since 2014 in the US. The detection is the first for the state of Montana, and is also the first for a wild bird in the Central Flyway.
