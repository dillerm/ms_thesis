H5N1 in Wild Birds in Russia:
Antibodies to, or genetic material of, H5 avian influenza  has been detected in 41 samples from wild migrant birds collected in Russia.  Antibodies to H5 or H7 were also detected in 19 samples from wild migrant birds  collected in three districts of the Tomsk Region. It is unclear if any of the  avian influenza viruses detected are highly pathogenic or are the low  pathogenic types commonly found in wild birds.
